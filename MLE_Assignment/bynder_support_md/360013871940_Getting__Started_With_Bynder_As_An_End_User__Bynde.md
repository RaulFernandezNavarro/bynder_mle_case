Welcome to Bynder! This guide is designed to help you quickly and easily navigate your Bynder portal and walk you through the essential features and tools you'll need to get started. By the end of this guide, you can confidently access your content and make the most of Bynder's powerful digital asset management capabilities.

What Is A DAM[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013871940-Getting-Started-With-Bynder-As-An-End-User#h_01J7ED2DGYBNP7FJDS9RC67BGS)

A digital asset management (DAM) system like Bynder is designed to help companies efficiently store, organize, and share digital assets such as images, videos, documents, and other multimedia content.

How Does Bynder Benefit Me[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013871940-Getting-Started-With-Bynder-As-An-End-User#h_01J7ED3AEPEG8VN61C1XK9ARH4)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bynderʼs digital asset management (DAM) is a record system for all brand, campaign, and product-related assets. With its AI-powerful functionality, intuitive UI, and rich set of integrations, it helps global teams store, manage, find, and deliver assets efficiently for a world-class customer content experience.

With Bynder DAM, you don't have to search through multiple folders[\*](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013871940-End-User-Guide-to-Bynder#:~:text=*Bynder%20uses%20a,you%20need%20easier.)
 or systems to find what you need. Collaborating and sharing assets with external customers, agencies, or partners is also easy. Bynder ensures quick and hassle-free access to on-brand and up-to-date assets.

\*Bynder uses a taxonomy structure instead of a traditional folder structure. This approach is more dynamic and flexible, allowing you to organize, manage, and retrieve digital assets without the limitations of a folder-based system. With taxonomy, you can filter thousands of assets by specific tags, making finding what you need easier.

Depending on how your Bynder Portal Admin has configured your account, you can log into your portal in two ways.

### Logging in via SSO (Single Sign On)

Once you navigate to your Bynder portal, click the **SSO** **login** **button**. You will then be automatically redirected to your company’s identity provider (e.g., Okta, SAML, Google).

### Email Activation

Check your email to see if you receive a message from your Bynder Admin, and select **Activate** **Now**.

If your organization does not have Single Sign On enabled, you may need to **create a password** and enter additional details required by your Bynder Admin. We suggest using a password with eight or more characters in combination with uppercase, numbers, letters, and special characters.

Once you have successfully logged in, you will be greeted by the Bynder dashboard. Here are the key areas you need to know.

*   **Top Navigation Bar:** Access various features like Assets and Collections, and if you have other solutions enabled (Studio, Brand Guidelines, Content Workflow, etc.).![Screenshot 2024-09-10 at 1.59.44 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21287585255442)
*   **Search Bar:** Quickly search for assets and Collections.![Screenshot 2024-09-10 at 2.00.00 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21287558304274)
*   **Quicklinks:** The Admin sets up these to provide quick access to important information, such as links to the Bynder Knowledge Base, external resources, or internal content within the DAM.![Screenshot 2024-02-08 at 2.21.15 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21287118199442)
*   **News Section:** This section provides updates on portal news posted by the Admin. Occasionally, your Admin may feature new collections in this section.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21287094909458)
*   **Bynder Express:** If this is enabled in your portal, you can share files (that do not live in the DAM) (up to 40 GB) via email or link with internal or external users. It may appear either at the top or bottom of your homepage.![Screenshot 2024-09-10 at 2.00.45 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21287585262866)

Your Bynder Admin has configured your DAM to make searching for valuable and up-to-date assets easy.  
If enabled, Bynder search has advanced AI capabilities to help you find the right resource with little effort. Your Bynder Admin controls what users can and cannot see, so you may not see certain assets your colleague can see. 

You can search for assets in three different ways:

*   **Smart Filters**
*   **Search Bar**
*   **Combination Smart Filters and Search Bar**: We recommend _applying filters first_ and then adding a search term in the search bar. You can save your filters for future access depending on whether your Admin has this permission enabled for you.

### Downloading

The great thing about the Bynder DAM is that you can download assets in a snap.

*   You can download one or more assets stored in your Asset Bank.
    *   If your Admin sets up the **download request form**, you may be required to fill out the download request form before downloading an asset.
    *   You can also request to **download assets with a Watermark** applied. Your Admin will have to approve your request before you can download it.

### Sharing Assets

You can share your assets with internal or external individuals, permission profiles, or groups.

If you want to share one or more assets, you can create a Collection, or if you have permission, you can send links to public Collections.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21287094912914)

_Note: You can customize the Collection permissions, such as allowing the user to download the assets, view them, or download any assets with a watermark applied (if configured)._
