# Asset Workflow Assets – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932879-Asset-Workflow-Assets

You can upload and download assets in Asset Workflow. Also, you can upload new versions to existing Workflow assets and compare them with each other. Learn more below on how you can upload and manage your assets in Workflow jobs.

Managing assets is permission-based. Go to **Settings > Users & rights > Permission Management** and verify your Workflow permissions.
