### AI

**AI Search Experience Now In UCV**

Bynder’s Universal Compact View (UCV) is a configurable, pre-built UI component providing a window into your DAM for searching and selecting assets from within another system. This new version now supports Natural Language and Similarity Search, making it easier and faster to find assets using the power of Bynder's AI.  
[**Edit Speech-to-Text Transcripts**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14713807155090-Speech-to-Text-for-Video-and-Audio-Assets)

For customers on Bynder’s AI Search Experience package, Bynder’s suite of AI-powered features helps you find and manage content faster – you can now edit AI-generated transcripts to correct brand names and keywords that AI may have misspelled. This ability to edit gives you more control and improves search accuracy.

**More Improvements For AI Search Experience**

AI Search Experience package users can now tag faces in Face Recognition by simply clicking on them. Natural Language Search is available from the Homepage, and Similarity Search from the Asset Detail View Page, making search faster and more intuitive.

### CXUC

[**Branded Template Library**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4410619103634-How-To-Create-and-Manage-Themes-in-CX-For-User-Community)
   
Templates now automatically reflect your brand’s theme for a consistent, polished look from the start. All widgets are now integrated into the theme set, allowing you to apply consistent styling to your guide or landing page.

### CXOC

[**Advanced Video Player Controls**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23906839206546-Adaptive-Video-Streaming)

Enhance the playback experiences of your videos with customizable video player settings. Configure settings like auto play, looping, hide controls and setting subtitle visibility.

[**Next-Gen Image Format Default Now AVIF**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/24571908724754-Understanding-Transformations-In-DAT)

Dynamic Asset Transformations (DAT) assets will now deliver images in an AVIF format by default when supported by the browser. AVIF offers a more efficient file format for optimal web performance.

### Studio

[**Basic PDF Export In Studio**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/16303105357330-How-To-Export-A-Design-In-Studio)

Export and share your Studio designs as a PDF for quick reviews, approvals, and content maintenance, keeping your designs polished and ready for every audience. 

### Asset Workflow

[**Bulk Actions In Job Overview**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360015363199-Custom-Job-Overview-in-Asset-Workflow)
   
Quickly update multiple jobs at once - move them to another campaign, reassign users, and adjust deadlines in just a few clicks. Keep projects on track, ensure accuracy, and free up time to focus on what matters most.

### DAM

[**New Homepage Experience**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210-How-To-Create-Your-Portal-Homepage)

Transform every DAM visit - internal or external - into a meaningful interaction with a dynamic and rich Homepage. Customize it fully to your brand and build a more engaging experience. Easily refresh layouts with every brand update, embed the latest assets and videos, and track performance with Analytics for smarter updates.

[**Similar Image Search Now Available With Bynder's AI-Powered Duplicate Manager**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23257091619090-How-To-Use-Bynder-s-AI-Powered-Duplicate-Manager)

The Duplicate Manager now identifies both exact and similar image duplicates, making it easier to keep your DAM duplicate-free. In addition, we are introducing a new, refreshed interface, more screen space, and fewer clicks. It’s a faster, more intuitive way to manage duplicates at scale and keep your DAM well-organized.

**New Toolbar Placement In Asset Overview**

We’ve moved the Toolbar in Asset Overview to the bottom center for greater visibility. It’s now easier to spot and quicker to access – bringing key actions closer to where you’re already focused and saving time in your daily workflows. Please note that this functionality will be released gradually to our customer base.

[**Custom Links Dashboards**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26827796589586-Understanding-The-Custom-Links-Dashboard-In-Analytics)

You can now better understand how your content is performing. Track clicks and creation data of the custom link, with breakdowns by country, user profile, and device usage. Gain insights into audience engagement, optimize distribution strategies, and measure the real impact of your content.
