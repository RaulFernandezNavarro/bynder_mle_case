# Search for Assets in your Asset Bank – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360

You can search for assets in your Bynder portal in two ways. You can use the [search bar](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360#h_01HGKM23M0PTAMSGKE2PH0JFCW)
 to find assets, collections, and Brand Guidelines or [search by filters](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360#h_01HGKME0FHBTFJWM2398EMEGBV)
 if you're not exactly sure what you're looking for or simply want to filter your content. We always recommend searching by filter first and then searching by term.

You can enhance your search capabilities if [AI Search Experience](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407741633426)
 is enabled in your portal. ![Screenshot 2023-12-01 at 4.20.11 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15487370059922)

 Search via Search Bar[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360#h_01JCRQ8RP7795FWVAZXB7G6HN1)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to your Asset Bank.
2.  Enter a term in the **search** **bar** to search for an asset. The system looks for the word in titles, descriptions, tags, and collection names. It can also scan the text in PDFs and other text files. You can learn more about [using the search bar to find assets.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931359)
    
3.  You can narrow your selection by [using filters](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875420)
    .

Search via Filters[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360#01JCRQ9CP5DJR60255N675ZNP9)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to your Asset Bank.
2.  You can begin filtering your search by clicking on the various options in your asset bank. 
3.  You can combine multiple filters for more accurate results. 
4.  You can learn more [here](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870120)
     by searching for assets through a filter. 

Search Using Both[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360#h_01HGKMHJWRT0NX2M18CEYEDW3C)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can also use this combination to find assets. We recommend applying filters first and then adding a search bar term.
