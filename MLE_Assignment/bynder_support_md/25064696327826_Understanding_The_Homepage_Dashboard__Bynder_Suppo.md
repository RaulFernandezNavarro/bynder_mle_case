Summary[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25064696327826-Understanding-The-Homepage-Dashboard#h_01K3TNP6P1C0CWNZDMGW2NEWE0)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Homepage Dashboard provides valuable insights into how users interact with the portal's homepages, including targeted homepages. 

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25064696327826-Understanding-The-Homepage-Dashboard#h_01K3TNS5ABMA91JQHK7HMR75VN)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This feature/solution requires your Customer Success Contact to enable, but then individual permissions can be done by the Bynder Admin.

Once _Advanced Analytics_ is enabled users need the _View advanced reporting_ permission to access this dashboard.

Why?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25064696327826-Understanding-The-Homepage-Dashboard#h_01K3TNX7906C9D0ER9R0X84J67)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

By tracking key metrics we can identify the content that captures users' attention, understand what they value most, monitor homepage interactions, and detect trends over time. This information allows Admins to make informed decisions to keep your homepage engaging, easy to navigate and aligned with user interests, ensuring it stays fresh, relevant, and effective.

How?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25064696327826-Understanding-The-Homepage-Dashboard#h_01K3TP18CDDBA5MYW07KZPF205)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Accessing the Homepage dashboard is easy. Simply click the settings wheel, scroll down to Reporting then select Analytics. From here users can select the dashboard in the left-hand side menu.

Key Metrics Provided[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25064696327826-Understanding-The-Homepage-Dashboard#h_01JNEMZZNKRJVA4S5YADKP9GXG)

### Total Homepage Views 

The total number of times your homepage is viewed gives you a general sense of its popularity.

### By Targeted Homepage

If you’ve set up multiple Homepages using Targeted Homepages, the data will be broken down into the separate pages.

### By Device 

Check which devices people use to view your homepage (e.g., desktop, tablet, or mobile). This helps you optimize your homepage for the devices people prefer.

### Unique Users Viewing the Homepage

This metric tells how many people visit your homepages during a selected time frame. It helps you understand how many users (not just repeated visits) engage with your homepage.

### User Activity 

This section shows the number of active users on your homepages and how much users engage with the content.

### By User Group 

See which groups (e.g., departments, external partners) most interact with your homepage.

### By User Profile 

Check how specific roles (e.g., managers and employees) engage with the homepage. This helps you understand how different audiences use your content.
