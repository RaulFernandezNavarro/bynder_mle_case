# Customize Portal Look and Feel – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936259

You can tailor the appearance of your portal to reflect your brand identity. While every portal includes a standard theme by default, upgrading to a custom theme offers enhanced branding options. Customizations will apply across the Asset Bank, Collections, [CX User Community](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/sections/21860874894610-Themes)
, and Asset Workflow solutions. Studio and Content Workflow currently do not support customization.

**Who can customize a Portal's Look & Feel?**

Standard Theme[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936259#h_01JAX7EWD4TDCZN13CDF7MQ1V5)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Updating the look and feel of your Bynder DAM is one way to make your portal stand out. Within our standard theme, you can update the [logo](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360017187080)
, [colors](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/10877538928914)
, [fonts](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/10878020652946)
, [favicon](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/10878138411154)
, and [login page buttons and copy](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6602034637202)
 to ensure that your portal is on-brand. 

### Note

We’ve released this new self-service section for updating the look and feel to a limited number of Bynder portals. If you are interested in switching to the self-service look and feel functionality, please contact your Customer Success Contact.
