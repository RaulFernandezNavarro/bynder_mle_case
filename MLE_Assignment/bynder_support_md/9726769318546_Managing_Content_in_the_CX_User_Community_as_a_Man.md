# Managing Content in the CX User Community as a Manager or Creator – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/9726769318546-Managing-Content-in-the-CX-User-Community-as-a-Manager-or-Creator

The CX User Community Overview page serves as a dedicated area for content management within Landing Pages and Guides. The sidebar that appears in the overview is only visible to managers. 

You can [customize the overview page](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/22050095256978)
 to display your Guides and Landing Pages in a grid or list view.

**Can those with [viewer permission](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433250182802-Permissions-in-CX-User-Community-Brand-Guidelines#:~:text=for%20portal%20administrators.-,Viewer,-Users%20with%20this)
 access the CX User Community overview page?** 

No, users with Viewer permissions cannot access the overview page. The design ensures that viewers can only see the Landing Pages and Guides shared with them, without additional content. However, they can [customize their view](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/22050095256978)
 if needed.![Screenshot 2024-12-02 at 3.44.51 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23023197971474)
