# Access Rights & Visibility Admin Training Webinar – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21194578494226-Access-Rights-Visibility-Admin-Training-Webinar

This [webinar recording](https://academy.bynder.com/m/b3be2e952cf943f/original/Access-Rights-Visibility-Admin-Training.mp4)
 reviews managing access and visibility for your assets and metaproperties. Properly managing these aspects is essential for keeping your digital assets secure and organized, particularly when addressing security, legal, or rights-related requirements. Effective access controls help protect your assets and make it easier for your team to find and use what they need.

In this webinar, you'll learn:

*   **[Metaproperty Option Level Access](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013872840)
    :** How to control asset visibility using metaproperty details.
*   **Using [Advanced Rights](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/sections/19091793789330-Advanced-Rights)
    :** How to use [watermarking](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931499)
    , [archiving](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931059)
    , and [limited usage](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013869860)
     rights to manage access to your assets.
*   **Metaproperty Visibility:** How to adjust [metaproperty visibility](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/9739941477138)
     to enhance the user experience.
