# Dynamic Asset Transformation (DAT) On-Demand Training – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20373097654674

Discover how [Dynamic Asset Transformation (DAT](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18952775916562)
) can revolutionize your image management. Watch this training video to learn how to optimize and automate your processes effortlessly!

[In this training](https://academy.bynder.com/m/6fbfabb7db1abaae/original/DAT-Admin-Training-Webinar.mp4)
, we cover:

*   **[Overview of DAT](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18952775916562)
    :** Understand how DAT simplifies image serving and enhances web development.
*   [**Real-Time Transformations:**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18953104053266)
     Learn to use URL parameters for cropping, resizing, and other image modifications.
*   [**DAT Presets:**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/5071969529874)
     Discover how to create and apply predefined transformations for various use cases.
*   [**Focus Points:**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18953630278290)
     Master setting and utilizing focus points for accurate cropping.
*   **[Integration & API](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/sections/360004091199-Bynder-Integrations)
    :** Explore how to connect DAT with CMSs and custom integrations through our API.
*   [**Permissions & Management:**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433257077906)
     Get insights on configuring user permissions and managing DAT settings.
