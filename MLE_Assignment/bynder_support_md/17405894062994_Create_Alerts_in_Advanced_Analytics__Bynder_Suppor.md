# Create Alerts in Advanced Analytics – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17405894062994-Create-Alerts-in-Advanced-Analytics

Alerts in [Advanced Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14829008886930)
 allow you to monitor important changes in your DAM system and take appropriate action. You can set up alerts based on specific metrics, determine thresholds, specify how often to check the data and choose who receives notification emails.

These alerts are useful for notifying key stakeholders about certain events, like a specific number of downloads on your platform, sudden decreases in user logins, or unsuccessful searches.

Find out [how to automate sending dashboard reports](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17406736018066)
 to Bynder users and non-users.

Keep in mind that there are ongoing improvements to the dashboards that might impact your alerts. Check the [Bynder Release Notes](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013872880)
 for any updates or enhancements to [Advanced Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14829008886930)
.

### Who can create alerts in Advanced Analytics?

Users with the following permission can create alerts in Advanced Analytics.

*   **View advanced reporting**

### Who can view alerts?

*   **Any external or internal Bynder user who is subscribed to the alert**

Create a New Alert[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17405894062994-Create-Alerts-in-Advanced-Analytics#h_01HQXN751S76PW2Y97JHYP55KK)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to **Settings** > **Reporting** > **Analytics**.
2.  Navigate to the dashboard where you would like to create an alert. ![Screenshot 2024-03-01 at 1.55.40 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17405894044946)
3.  Select ![Bell - notifications.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17405847828626)
4.  Enter the details for your alert:
    *   **Name**: Enter a name for your alert.
    *   **Condition**: Click the dropdowns to select which conditions will trigger the alert. This allows you to set an alert on a breakdown of a number.
        *   For example, if you want to set an alert to know when more than 100 assets have been downloaded, you would set up an alert if the **Downloaded** **Assets** is greater than or equal to 100, and the individual will receive a notification.
    *   Select from the dropdown options based on how your Portal is configured (i.e., **Any Assets, Published, Pending, Rejected).**
    *   Select is **greater than, less than, equal to, greater than, equal to, or less than or equal to**.
    *   Enter a **Value**.
5.  **Where to send it**: In the field below Email, you can just type in the email address(s) to which you would like to receive alerts. These can be Bynder or non-Bynder users. 
    *   Press the TAB or ENTER key to include more email addresses.
6.  **Frequency**: Select the frequency of the alerts: **Monthly, Weekly, or Daily, so as** not to overwhelm the notification receiver.
7.  **Time**: Select the time of day that the alerts will be sent.
    *   The user's time zone needs to be respected. US Pacific timezone will be used.
8.  Click **Save** **Alert.** ![Screenshot 2024-03-01 at 1.56.23 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17405847829650)
9.  Once the condition is met, the alert will trigger an email to the configured email address(es) and include the associated dashboard in your Portal.

**Can I unsubscribe from an alert that someone created?**  
If you want to be removed from receiving alerts, you must notify the creator of the alert to remove you from receiving them.

**Can I follow someone else's alerts that they've created?**  
No, you cannot follow someone's alerts; you can only see the alerts you set up in your Portal.
