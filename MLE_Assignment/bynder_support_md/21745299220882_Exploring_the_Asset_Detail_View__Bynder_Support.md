# Exploring the Asset Detail View – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View

The Asset Detail View provides a comprehensive overview of an individual [asset](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016051239)
 metadata, version history, permissions, and usage. This view is crucial for understanding asset-specific details, making edits, tracking usage, and managing rights.

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21766616778898)

Metadata[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View#h_01J0BJ95JSB705EZMM38QE3ZRQ)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Metadata](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17636258833042)
 is crucial for organizing and retrieving digital assets efficiently. In the asset detail view, you can view associated [**metaproperty fields**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6190351879186)
, [**automated** **tags,**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/16841116279570)
 **copyright** **information**, and [**custom** **fields**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931199)
 specific to your organization. Properly tagging and categorizing assets enhances searchability and ensures consistent usage across projects.

Usage Rights and Permissions[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View#h_01J0BJ9JDPQ2QSVDE5TWZCDQXN)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Understanding an asset's [**usage rights and permissions**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433246503442)
 is essential for compliance purposes. The asset detail may include information on **licensing**, **usage** **restrictions**, and **expiration** **dates**.

Download Options[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View#h_01J0BJ9QZMJRYNPTESE3NN4R56)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can view and download the [**related assets,**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931679)
 [**variations**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931139)
, [**derivatives**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013869940)
, or [**DAT transformations**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360018559260)
 **if enabled.**

Sharing Options[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View#h_01J0BJ9ZM1GE6WMHSPJ9RJGTPB)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Options for sharing the asset may include a [**link to a file**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870240)
 or an option to [**embed media**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870040)
**.** [](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870040)

Versions and History[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View#h_01J0BJ9C07B1HYT102KFT5Y8HF)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The [**Versions**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931859)
 tab is where you can view the version history, compare changes, and restore previous versions if you have the right to do so.

Modify Asset[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View#01J0BMTK29671HMYBTT7HPFZSJ)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If available, you can change an [asset's focus point.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931299)
 If you have permission, you can edit the related asset information in the [**Edit**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870340)
 tab.

Asset Statistics[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882-Exploring-the-Asset-Detail-View#h_01J0BNG60CMSGJ7XG593DPGKJJ)
