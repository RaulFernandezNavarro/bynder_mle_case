# Schedule Automatic Report Delivery in Advanced Analytics – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17406736018066-Schedule-Automatic-Report-Delivery-in-Advanced-Analytics

In [Advanced Analytics,](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14829008886930)
 you can automate the distribution of reports and dashboards directly to users' emails. You have control over the recurrence (minutes, hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, specific days or months, or based on data updates), who can receive the dashboard, and what format they will receive it in (CSV, PDF, or PNG).

This ensures that all relevant Bynder and non-Bynder users receive the latest data and insights at a predefined time without manual intervention.

Learn how to [create alerts](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17405894062994)
 to be sent to Bynder users and non-users to notify them when specific metrics or thresholds have been met in your portal. 

_Please note that there are continuous improvements to the dashboards that may affect or alter your automatic reports. Please check [Bynder Release Notes](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013872880)
 for any improvements or updates to [Advanced Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14829008886930)
._

### Who can schedule automatic reports in Advanced Analytics?

Users with the following permission can schedule automatic reports in Advanced Analytics.

*   **View advanced reporting**

### Who can view automatic reports in Advanced Analytics?

*   **Any user, whether internal or external to Bynder, who receives the report**

1.  Navigate to **Settings** > **Reporting** > **Analytics**.
2.  Navigate to the report that you would like to schedule the delivery for.
3.  Click ![Screenshot 2024-02-09 at 3.48.10 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17406736014738) in the upper right-hand corner and select **Schedule** **Delivery**.![Screenshot 2024-03-01 at 2.32.39 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17406736015890)
4.  Under **Settings**, enter the details for the Schedule Delivery.![Screenshot 2024-03-01 at 2.39.09 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17406773219474)  
    *   **Schedule Name**: Enter a name for your report.
    *   **Recurrence**: Set Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Specific Days, or Months based on Data Updates.
    *   **Time**: Select from the dropdown menu to indicate the delivery time.
    *   **Destination**: Select **Email** from the dropdown.
    *   **Email** **addresses**: Enter the email address(es) for whom you want to receive the report.  
        Recipients can be Bynder users or external to Bynder. **Limited to 50 email addresses.**
        *   Press the TAB or ENTER key to include more email addresses.
    *   **Format**: Select the file format from the dropdown. This will be the file type in the attachment in the Email. 
        *   **PDF** (typically used for presentations)
        *   **CSV** (typically used for data analysis)
        *   **PNG** (the image of the dashboard will appear directly in the Email).
    *   Under **Filters,** select from the dropdown menu for dynamic values based on how your portal is configured. For example, this can be **Asset** **Type** or **File** **Extension**. ![Screenshot 2024-03-01 at 2.41.22 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17406736016274)
        *   (Optional) **Advanced** **Options:** you can select from the following:
            *   Include **Custom** **Message**
            *   **Include Links** (Disregard this option as it does not produce any links at the moment) 
            *   Select **Expand tables to show all rows**
            *   Select **Arrange dashboard tiles in a single column**
            *   Select from the dropdown the **Paper size**
            *   Select from the dropdown menu to indicate the **Delivery timezone ![Screenshot 2024-03-01 at 2.42.12 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/17406773220754)** 
5.  Click **Save**.
6.  The report will be sent to the email address(es) indicated in the setup.

Note: As the schedule creator, you can select **Test** **Now** (located at the bottom of your screen). A test report will be sent to the Email indicated in the recipient list.
