# Adjust Time Frame Filters in Analytics Dashboards – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20047789830290-Adjust-Time-Frame-Filters-in-Analytics-Dashboards

In many of the [Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932579)
 dashboards for [Light](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14379638195858)
, [Standard](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14713961721106)
, and [Advanced](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14829008886930)
, you can adjust the **Time** **Frame** or view the data in a graph for a **specific** **month** using the time frame filters. In addition you can control the Date Granularity for a closer look into the data. For example, you might want to check the asset view and downloads on the day of a new campaign launch or for a particular month/year. Follow the steps below to adjust the time frame filters on your dashboard to suit your needs.

How to Adjust Time Frame Filters [](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20047789830290-Adjust-Time-Frame-Filters-in-Analytics-Dashboards#h_01J2C5EQR1WV9AFJCGANWYFJHQ)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  In the **Time** **Frame** section of the dashboard, click on the box to access the dropdown options.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/20047789825298)
2.  Choose your desired time frame from the dropdown menus.
    *   Several options are available. The most commonly used ones are **is in the last**, **is on the day**, and **is in the range.**
3.  Click ![Screenshot 2023-11-30 at 2.36.44 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/20047782460946) in the top right-hand corner to apply the new time frame to the dashboard.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/20094472089234)

Understanding the Filter Period[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20047789830290-Adjust-Time-Frame-Filters-in-Analytics-Dashboards#h_01J2C5BBV3XXJ3GZ55R599A7R5)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   The filter period is calculated based on the end of the full period, which depends on the granularity of your data.
*   If you are selecting a date within the range, make sure you select a day further than what you want. For example, if you want to see the metrics for this date range, **March 15, 2024** - **July 1, 2024**, make sure you select **July 2**.
*   The filter always includes the current period up to its end.
    *   For example, if you select the last two years, the filter will show the **Current** **Calendar** **Year** and the **Previous** **Calendar** **Year**.
    *    If today is in July and you select the last three quarters, the filter will display data from **January** until **October**, as October marks the end of the current quarter. 
    *   To view the data for a specific month, hover your mouse over the line on the graph.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/20047782465042)
