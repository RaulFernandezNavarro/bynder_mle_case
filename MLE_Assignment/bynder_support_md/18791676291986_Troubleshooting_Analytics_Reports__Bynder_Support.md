# Troubleshooting Analytics Reports – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18791676291986-Troubleshooting-Analytics-Reports

If you are experiencing difficulties [viewing your dashboards](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14465131283218)
 in [Bynder Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932579)
, it could be due to your web browser. We always recommend using the latest version of [Google Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/)
 or [Firefox](https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/)
.

If you use Safari, Edge, or Internet Explorer, note that they block third-party cookies automatically. [Choose your browser from the list](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18791676291986-Troubleshooting-Analytics-Reports#01HXF72HH4JRW4DGG62FTW2HFZ)
 below to learn how to enable third-party cookies. 

Recommended Browsers[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18791676291986-Troubleshooting-Analytics-Reports#h_01HXF6WMV060G22PV3FTN8ZSPP)

**I am encountering a 401 error whenever I attempt to access Analytics.**

You may need to enable cookies in your browser. Find your browser and [learn how to enable cookies](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18791676291986-Troubleshooting-Analytics-Reports#01HXF72HH4JRW4DGG62FTW2HFZ)
.

How to Enable Third-Party Cookies[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18791676291986-Troubleshooting-Analytics-Reports#01HXF72HH4JRW4DGG62FTW2HFZ)

### Chrome

1.  Open Chrome.
2.  Open **Privacy** **settings** by going to **chrome://settings/cookies** in your address bar.
3.  Check if Block third-party cookies are enabled and change the setting to **Allow third-party cookies**.
4.  Restart your browser to start using Bynder Analytics.

### Safari

1.  Open Safari.
2.  Click **Safari** > **Preferences** in the top bar.
3.  Open the **Privacy** **tab** and ensure the **Prevent** **cross**\-**site** **tracking** and **Block** **all** **cookies** options are **disabled**.
4.  Restart your browser to start using **Bynder** **Analytics**.

### Edge

1.  Open Edge.
2.  Click **![Screenshot 2024-03-05 at 9.37.43 AM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/18791676282642)** **More** in the top right corner of the browser > **Settings**.
3.  Go to **Cookies and site permissions,** then **Manage and delete cookies and site data**.
4.  Turn off **Block third-party cookies**. 

### Internet Explorer

1.  Open Internet Explorer.
2.  Click the **Settings** **Icon** in the top right corner of the screen and click Internet **options**.
3.  Open the **Privacy** **tab,** and in the **Settings** section, click **Advanced**.
4.  In the Third-party Cookies section, select **Accept**.
5.  Click **OK.**
6.  Click **Apply** to apply the changes, and click **OK** to close the screen.
7.  Restart your browser to start using **Bynder** **Analytics**.
