Innovation is at the heart of Bynder’s values. We are always working to enhance and modernize our products. Customer feedback is one of the most effective ways to identify areas for improvement or new features.

The Bynder Roadmap[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406445265938#h_01JQ4DBKV7JHRM6Y3V3CWNX71W)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

As a Bynder customer, you can access our Product Roadmap within our [Community](https://community.bynder.com/)
.

**If this is your first visit, you must [create an account.](https://community.bynder.com/welcome-get-started-45/how-to-create-your-account-on-the-community-615?tid=615&fid=45)
**

Your feedback shapes the Bynder Roadmap and offers transparency into our product development process. It allows you to see what we’ve released, what we’re currently working on, and what we are considering building next.

How To Share Feedback[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406445265938#h_01JQ4DCMZNADCN5GRPJ7S0KA32)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We encourage you to participate in discussions within the Bynder Community. Here, you can vote on the Product Roadmap and express the importance of specific ideas.

You can also share detailed product feedback with your Customer Success Contact or reach out to Support.

We invite you to join our upcoming webinars, where the Bynder team will showcase new releases and upcoming plans and actively seek feedback from our customers.

Additionally, you can participate in product feedback sessions, which we’ll announce during roadmap webinars, through conversations with your Customer Support Contact, or as part of beta testing programs.

Recommended Feedback Structure[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406445265938#h_01JQQDEXX5ZQFRH5SMYNTCW6NB)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Please use the structure below when sharing feedback via the Product Roadmap, with your Customer Success Contact, or with the Support team. This will help our team understand your needs more clearly. 

"As a **\[user\]** from **\[Business Name\]**, I **\[want to...\], \[so that...\].** This is **\[degree at which it is important, i.e., _nice to have, necessary, etc..._\]":**

*   **\[user\]** describes the type of user in Bynder, not your title.
    
*   **\[want to\]** outline the user's intent—what are you trying to achieve? Do not describe the UI here.
    
*   **\[so that\]** refers to the bigger picture being addressed.
    

Once we receive your feedback, it will be shared with our Product team for review and consideration for inclusion in the roadmap.

**Every piece of feedback is reviewed and valued.**
