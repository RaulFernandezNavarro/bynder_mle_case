# Users & Storage (Cockpit) – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936199-Users-Storage-Cockpit

The **Users & Storage** section under your **Advanced** **Settings** shows the number of users active, and the number of user seats that are still available. In some portals, this section may be called **Cockpit**. 

### Tip

Previously, Cockpit showed your storage; however, now you can see how much storage each of your Bynder solutions takes up in [Bynder Analytics.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932579)
 

![Screenshot 2024-05-07 at 3.03.40 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/18795698932882)

### How to add additional users and storage to your account?

This feature/solution requires your Customer Success Contact to enable, but then individual permissions can be done by the Bynder Admin.

**Who can access Users & Storage section?**

Users with the following permissions can manage to add, remove, or edit users:

*   [View Users & Storage](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433246503442-Permissions-in-Asset-Bank#:~:text=View%20Users%20%26%20Storage%20%2D%20Allows%20users%20to%20view%20the%20portal%27s%20storage%20and%20user%20licenses.)
     

How to Access Users & Storage Section (Cockpit)[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936199-Users-Storage-Cockpit#h_01HXS8MZ3904G1KE5KK4MRHQZG)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to your Portal.
2.  Select **Settings > Advanced Settings> Users & Storage.** 
    *   In some portals, this section may be called **Cockpit**.
3.  Here, you can check the number of user seats you have, broken down by user type. It also shows you how many are currently used.

Previously, Cockpit showed your storage; however, now you can see how much storage each of your Bynder solutions takes up in [Bynder Analytics.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932579)
