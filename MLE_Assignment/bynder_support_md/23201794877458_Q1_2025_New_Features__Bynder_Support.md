### **Bynder AI**

[**AI Search Experience, now available via API**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407741633426-Bynder-s-AI-Search-Experience-Offerings)

Discover content faster, easier, and more intuitively. Bynder’s AI-powered capabilities, such as Natural Language Search, Search by Image, and Similarity Search, can now be accessed via API. Let users describe assets in their preferred language or explore the DAM using an image – all directly from your CMS or other internal tools.  
  

  
![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186358226194)

### **DAM**

**[New file format supported](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936359-Understanding-Preview-Supported-File-Types)
 – [HEIC](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875220-Understanding-Supported-File-Types-For-Conversion-And-Derivative-Creation)
**

We’ve added HEIC to the list of image formats supported for asset preview in the Bynder DAM. You can now view thumbnails and full previews of HEIC files without downloading them, which makes it easier to browse, find, and reuse content from Apple devices.

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186321990034)

[**CSV Metadata Export just got better**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/22486522910994-How-To-Download-A-CSV-File-With-All-Metadata)

You asked for it – we delivered! We've added highly requested fields like Asset ID, Advanced Rights, Published Date, Archive Date, and Modified By to the CSV metadata export. Now it’s easier than ever to audit, report, and analyze your assets.

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186358228498)

[**Collection Groups**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26007812298642-How-To-Create-Collection-Groups)

Create groups for your collections to more easily organize and share with your stakeholders. Once created, you can easily share a link that makes it easier to find relevant assets. Admins and permitted users can make customized groups visible to specific users or the entire portal, improving collection discoverability for your organization.

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186358229010)

### **CX User Community**

[**Built-in Image Cropper**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/10436495883282-Recommended-Image-Sizes-for-CX-User-Community)

Crop and resize images directly in your Guides and Landing Pages - no pre-editing needed! The Image Cropper works wherever images are used, from cover images to headers to cards, making it easy to get the right fit and make your content shine. 

**![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186321992594)**

### **CX Omnichannel**

**Limited-use & Archived states for CX Omnichannel links**

Further protect your proprietary assets by controlling when they should be publicly available through CX Omnichannel links. Now you can set an archive date or release date(“limited-use”) to easily manage visibility of assets on your digital channels.

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186321992978)

### **Content Operations**

[**Gradients in Studio**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25923547221394-How-To-Create-And-Use-Gradients-In-Studio)

Enhance your creativity with Studio’s new Gradient support. Apply linear or radial gradients to shapes, text, and backgrounds, fine-tune colors and opacity, and create brand-consistent designs—all within Studio.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186321994130)

### **Analytics**

[**AI Search Dashboard**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25834920685714-Understanding-The-AI-Search-Dashboard-In-Advanced-Analytics)

Gain insights into how your company leverages AI Search with the new AI Search Dashboard. Track usage by search type, user group, and individual users to identify top performers and areas for improvement—helping you maximize AI-driven content discovery.

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26186321994514)

### DAM

#### Natural Language Search

Now, [AI Search Experience](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407741633426)
 customers can effortlessly locate assets using natural, everyday language, such as “man wearing a hat”, without needing specific keywords or knowledge of metadata. NLS understands intent and context, providing more relevant and accurate results in 100+ languages.

[Learn more here.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/22756721920274)

#### No More Duplicates in Your DAM!\*

Use the [Duplicate Image Finder](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870020)
 to detect exact _image_ duplicates in your DAM. If you have [AI Search Experience](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407741633426)
 enabled, you can use the [AI-powered Duplicate Manager](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23257091619090)
 to identify and manage duplicates across all file types.

\*Please note that this capability will be released on **January 27**.

#### Custom Links for Collections

Sharing your collections is now easier than ever! Create a short, memorable link that hints at what’s inside while keeping it on-brand. Want even simpler access? Generate a QR code instantly and share with ease!

[Learn more here.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23824094080914)

#### All Collections in One Overview

The "All collections" section makes finding what you need a breeze! You can search and browse all the collections you can access—no more hassle remembering owners.

\*Please note that this capability will be gradually rolled out starting on **January 26**.
