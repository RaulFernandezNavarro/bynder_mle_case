# How To Manage Your User Profile – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013933239-How-To-Manage-Your-User-Profile

### Summary

You can manage the details of your user profile. This will allow you to update your photo, personal details, and password yourself.

### Who?

All Bynder users should have access to this feature. 

_Note:_ Contact your portal administrator if this option isn't available for your account.

### Why?

Bynder users should have the freedom to update their personal details as they wish. We empower users to update details that won't affect their portal's security configuration. 

### How?

1.  Log in to your portal.
2.  Hover over your **username,** this is in the top right-hand corner of your portal navigation bar.
    
3.  Select the **Edit profile** option from the dropdown menu.
    
    ### ![Screenshot 2024-06-17 at 2.32.40 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/19596985563538)
    
4.  Update your profile information.
    

_Note:_ To change your email address or username, contact your system administrator. ![Screenshot 2024-06-17 at 2.35.38 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/19596985566610)

    5. Click **Save**.
