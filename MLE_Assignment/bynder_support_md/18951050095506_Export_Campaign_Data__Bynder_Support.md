# Export Campaign Data – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18951050095506-Export-Campaign-Data

You can generate a report detailing the number of Asset Workflow jobs created, their duration, and completion status. Additionally, you can extract more detailed analytics using the [Analytics Solution](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932579)
. Export the data from the Asset Workflow campaign and open the file in your preferred document viewer. Consider organizing the information into columns for better clarity. Each job in the exported campaign will be displayed in a separate row, identified by a unique Job Code. For jobs with multiple stages and uploaded assets, refer to the corresponding rows for specific details.

How to Export Campaign Data[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18951050095506-Export-Campaign-Data#h_01HXYJMEFR7ZR2V3GF6GTG35NC)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  In the campaign overview, hover over the Campaign for which you want to export data.
    
2.  Click the **Export** icon.
    
3.  Select the data that needs to be exported. You can also select the metaproperties you want to export.
    
    ![export-campaign-data.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/18951026790418)
    
4.  Click **Export** to generate a CSV file.
