# Notification Center in your Portal – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875280-Notification-Center-in-your-Portal

Our Notification Center keeps you in the loop about portal activity, informs you when your downloads are ready, and notifies you when tasks require your attention. 

### Note

The information below applies to the latest version of our [Notification Center](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875280-Notification-Center-in-your-Portal#UUID-7e182c15-f518-c468-3327-12c323ef0345 "Notification Center")
. Contact your Customer Success Contact if your version of the notification center looks different to discuss updating your portal to the latest version.

How to Mark Notifications Read[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875280-Notification-Center-in-your-Portal#h_01HMHTY2XNYH1J68HJ05EW50MP)

Once you have audited a request, downloaded your files, or when you no longer need notifications, you can remove them from the list.

1.  Click the ![notification_center.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10640557556626) in the top bar of your portal to open the Notification Center.
    
2.  Hover over a notification and click the ![delete_notification.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10640557593618) icon to remove the notification from the list.
    

Please Note: Audit Asset notifications are not automatically removed if an auditor has approved/disapproved an asset. You will still need to clear the notification manually.

Types of Notifications[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875280-Notification-Center-in-your-Portal#h_01HMHTY2XP7NR60ER3AS2VWDYK)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bynder supports three types of notifications. Users can receive in-app notifications or email notifications. Additionally, [SNS notifications](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875360)
 can be set up to send notifications to external systems. If you want to enable any or all of these notifications, please contact your Customer Success Contact.

### Notifications for new version uploads

Users who downloaded an asset before can receive an in-app notification when a new version is uploaded to that asset. This applies to the original asset as well as one of the derivatives.

### Notifications for archived assets

Users who downloaded an asset before can receive an in-app notification when that asset has been archived. 

### Notifications for new asset uploads, updated asset metadata, and deleted assets.

[SNS notifications](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875360)
 can be sent to an external system when a new asset has been uploaded, metadata has been updated, or an asset has been removed.
