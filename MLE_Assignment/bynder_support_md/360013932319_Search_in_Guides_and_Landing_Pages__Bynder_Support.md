# Search in Guides and Landing Pages – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932319-Search-in-Guides-and-Landing-Pages

The [CX User Community](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21861208799634)
 provides intuitive search and navigation, making exploring and locating content within [Guides](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932239)
 or [Landing Pages](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21908795341714)
 easy. Depending on your user type, selecting a Guide or Landing Page will direct you to the content where you can search using the [portal search bar](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932319-Search-in-Guides-and-Landing-Pages#h_01JDJDNXC5W8GF6ND8B4FA90C8)
 or display a navigation sidebar.

If you're [sent a link](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23021339513234)
 to a Guide or Landing Page with [**viewer**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433250182802-Permissions-in-CX-User-Community-Brand-Guidelines#:~:text=for%20portal%20administrators.-,Viewer,-Users%20with%20this)
 permissions, you may be taken directly to the content or certain sections. 

No matter your [permission](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433250182802)
 level, the search bar is always available to help you efficiently navigate and access content across Guides and Landing Pages.

How to Search for Content in Guides or Landing Pages[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932319-Search-in-Guides-and-Landing-Pages#h_01JDJDNXC5W8GF6ND8B4FA90C8)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  You can use the [DAM Search bar](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360)
     (located in your portal), which searches across **all** things in your Asset Bank and CX User Community, or navigate directly to the CX User Community tab (the name may vary in your portal).![Screenshot 2024-12-02 at 2.57.26 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23021884401426)
2.  Enter a **search** term into the search bar. ![Screenshot 2024-11-25 at 2.55.04 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/22863727553810)
3.  A results page will appear with related content. 

Search Tips[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932319-Search-in-Guides-and-Landing-Pages#h_01JDJGZBYJH7HX87Y5188CVT90)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can narrow the search by using the following filters:

*   The most relevant search results are listed at the top of the page.
*   Your search term is displayed in **bold text** so you can quickly determine if that result is from a guide or landing page you want to visit.
*   Clicking on the search result will redirect you to the page where that term lives.

*   Click **All** **results** to narrow your search to Groups, Guides, Pages, or Widgets.
*   Click **Any** **time** to narrow your search to a specific timeframe.

*   Your last **five recent searches** are saved in the search box dropdown.
*   **Guide Chapters** are currently not included in the search results.
