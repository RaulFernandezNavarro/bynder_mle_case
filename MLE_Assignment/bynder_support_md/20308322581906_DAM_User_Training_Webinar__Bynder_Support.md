# DAM User Training Webinar – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20308322581906-DAM-User-Training-Webinar

This [webinar recording](https://academy.bynder.com/m/6f6aad0f459e26bc/original/Bynder-User-Training-Webinar.mp4)
 is designed to help you get the most out of Bynder, whether you're a new user or need a quick refresher. 

In this training webinar, you will learn how to:

*   **Search and download assets:** Efficiently locate and retrieve the assets you need.
*   **Create and share collections:** Organize and collaborate on collections with ease.
*   **Upload assets:** Seamlessly add new assets to your library.
*   **Edit and update metadata:** Keep your asset information accurate and up-to-date.
