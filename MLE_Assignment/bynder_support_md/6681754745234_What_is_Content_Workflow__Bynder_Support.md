# What is Content Workflow – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6681754745234

Content Workflow simplifies the end-to-end management of structured text content, making it easy to scale your production efforts. Create, review, and approve content seamlessly—without the hassle of juggling documents, chasing feedback, or manually copying content into publishing tools.

Designed to handle high volumes of written content, the Content Workflow solution is perfect for everything from blog posts and articles to full website builds and complex marketing campaigns.

Whether you need it as a standalone solution or integrated directly within your Bynder portal, Content Workflow ensures your team stays organized and productive at every step.

This feature/solution requires your Customer Success Contact to enable, but then individual permissions can be done by the Bynder Admin.

What Can Content Workflow Do for You?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6681754745234#h_01JAX8EEKAP4BCRM7PA8EA39NV)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Content Workflow offers a robust suite of features designed to streamline your content operations. It enables you to bring high-quality content to market faster and more efficiently.

Structured Templates[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6681754745234#h_01JAX8EJQT74NKF1RED0XQ0307)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Easily [create templates](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/sections/20439968916114-Templates)
 for different content types, providing clear guidelines for authors and editors. This ensures consistency and helps you scale content production without compromising quality or brand standards.

Real-Time Collaboration[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6681754745234#h_01JAX8ESY5FWGA552H3A1KKZM2)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Collaborate](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/sections/18538713238802-Collaborate)
 with teammates and stakeholders in real-time, providing in-line feedback on the content. Use notifications and comment mentions to keep discussions focused and the editing process simple and organized.

Flexible Workflow Management[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6681754745234#h_01JAX8F1E2QV1FGQCYTK0JCVSD)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Move your content smoothly through [customizable workflow steps](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/sections/18929358239122-Create-Manage)
, ensuring all requirements are met before publication. This structured approach gives you confidence that nothing is missed, helping you maintain high standards.

Seamless Publishing Across Channels[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6681754745234#h_01JAX8F7R8Z8X3J3ZSGXXX7EMS)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

With direct integrations and [API](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14984593670034)
 support, Content Workflow lets you publish content wherever needed without extra steps. Create once and distribute effortlessly across multiple platforms.
