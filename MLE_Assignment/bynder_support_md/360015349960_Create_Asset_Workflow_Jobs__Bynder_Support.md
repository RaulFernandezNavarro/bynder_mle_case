# Create Asset Workflow Jobs – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360015349960-Create-Asset-Workflow-Jobs

Jobs often consist of multiple stages and help to streamline projects that are often part of a bigger Asset Workflow campaign. For example, product launches or asset or POD approvals. Jobs are often based on a preset, which are predefined stages and tasks. Presets allow users to easily work on their deliverables and at the same time ensure that stage responsible parties get the information they need. Find out how you can create a job below.

### Note

We advise to only create jobs that are based on a preset. Contact your Bynder Administrator if no preset is available.
