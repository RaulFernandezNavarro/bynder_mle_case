# How To Create Your Portal Homepage – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210

The Homepage enables you to create a fully customizable, self-service portal homepage designed to boost user engagement and drive portal adoption. Whether you’re building from [scratch](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JHG69SPJ46EZ40H3Y21793PS)
 or using [pre-built templates](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JHG69SPJF4ZK10DG0N0E2N4X)
, this feature provides the flexibility to craft visually appealing and functional designs tailored to your organization’s needs.

With Landing Pages, you can seamlessly embed assets directly from the DAM and customize layouts with brand-specific styling, ensuring an on-brand and user-friendly homepage experience.

If you have [Advanced Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14829008886930)
 enabled, you can access a [dedicated dashboard](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25064696327826)
 that tracks user interactions with your homepage—whether users are logged in or, in the case of an open asset bank, browsing without logging in. This data helps you understand what content resonates with users, identify areas for improvement, and optimize your homepage to create a visually engaging and intuitive portal that leaves a lasting impression and enhances the user experience within Bynder.

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JWTXXN674B08FZ3M60Y2HJCS)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This feature/solution is enable-able by any Bynder portal user.

Users who should be able to create or edit a Homepage need the _Manage Homepage_ permission. 

Before you Begin [](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JHG69SPJ4BGWZD9MAN1GF8JP)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   If you are replicating your existing homepage with the new and improved Landing Pages, we recommend transferring all essential content from your existing homepage to the new design to ensure a smooth transition.
*   If you haven't already, ensure that all your light users who need access to the published homepage have the [Viewer](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433250182802-Permissions-in-CX-User-Community-Brand-Guidelines#:~:text=for%20portal%20administrators.-,Viewer,They%20cannot%20add%20or%20edit%20any%20content%20in%20the%20respective%20solution.,-Custom%20Links%20Permissions)
     permission enabled in their permission profile.

Formatting Tips[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JHG69SPJ7PXYEYC6PWW9GZ55)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Keep the design clean and user-friendly.
*   Use the draft mode to review and gather feedback before publishing.
*   Use videos to engage your users.
*   Add spacing dividers for clarity.
*   Align typography and colors with your brand.

Create your Homepage using a Template[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JHG69SPJF4ZK10DG0N0E2N4X)

Streamline the process by starting with predesigned templates:

1.  Navigate to **domainname.bynder.com/guidelines/homepages**
2.  Select **+New Homepage.**
3.  Enter a **Title (“Homepage,” etc).**
4.  Choose from the pre-built templates. ![Screenshot 2025-08-15 at 14.04.00.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/28787987571090)
5.  Customize each section with your copy, branding, and theme.

7\. Add widgets or extra section templates for further customization.

Create your Homepage from Scratch[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JHG69SPJ46EZ40H3Y21793PS)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For more flexibility, build your homepage from the ground up. 

1.  Navigate to **CX User Community**.
2.  Select **Landing Pages.**
3.  Select **+New Landing Page.**
4.  Enter a **Title** (“Homepage,” etc).
5.  Select **Blank** **Template**.
6.  Start adding widgets by hovering over the page and selecting **+**.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901110388498)
7.  Use section templates (e.g., **banners**, **body**, **navigation**, **footer**) or add **blank** sections.
8.  See below for a step-by-step guide on how to build your homepage.

Section-by-Section Guide to Building Your Homepage[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210#h_01JHG69SPJDADKX4GQSRGK17GH)

### Add Intro/ Welcome Banner

1.  #### Select section template > **Banner (Header Banner).**![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901099094034)
    
2.  Add **Copy**
    *   Include a welcome message or portal purpose.
3.  Customize the background by adding an image, GIF, or solid color.
4.  Add redirect buttons to link to your portal's Asset Bank and other sections.
    *   For instance, you can link to a [specific metaproperty view](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/19524701689618-Create-Direct-Link-to-Custom-Metaproperty-Views)
         or a curated selection of assets [filtered by a keyword or tag.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/19524922463378-Create-Direct-Link-to-Specific-Asset-Keyword-or-Tag)
        
5.  (optional) Add a Secondary Redirect Button. This button redirects users to areas like external websites, your company’s Brand Guidelines, or [other Bynder solutions.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901099090322)](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875420-Create-Direct-Links-to-Specific-Solutions)
    

### Add Content 

#### Tip

Check the [**Analytics**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932579)
 solution/pages (e.g., search) to see what search terms your users are using, and based on this, create cards with redirect links.

1.  #### Select Section **Template** > **Body** > **Select** any of the body templates that fit your needs.
    
    ![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901110400402)
2.  **Add** **Copy**.
    *   Use cards to highlight your departments (marketing, sales, etc.), content types (image, video, etc.), content subtypes (logo, icons, powerpoints, etc.), campaigns (sprint, summer campaign), and products. 

#### ![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901099100050) ![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901110408210)

### Add Navigation or Footer Templates to serve as Quick Links.

Previously, you may have added [quick links](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360017005139)
 to your homepage to help users easily navigate to specific DAM sections or external resources. With the new homepage experience, these links can be elevated using **cards** or incorporating **footer** or **navigation templates** for a more visually engaging design.

1.  #### Select **Section** **Template** >**Navigation** or **Footer** template.
    

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901110426514)![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901099125138)

3.  **Add** a **Copy** to the Card or Button to explain if there is an action item, e.g., click here to download or access.
4.  Add **background** **color** or **image**.
5.  Add a **redirect** **link** to specific sections or content in your DAM or external resources for quicker access. 
    *   For example, you can link to Bynder support articles, end-user training resources, or even external HR links that can serve as a central hub for users to access everything in one place. ![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901110428818)![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901099135506)![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/23901110412050)

### Add Bynder Express

1.  Select a **button** or **card** widget
2.  Add **"https://yourportaldomain.com/express"**  to the custom link to give users easy access to the [Bynder Express](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21886575898386)
     widget.
