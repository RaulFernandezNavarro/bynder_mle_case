# Subtitles Support in Adaptive Video Streaming – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25061882331922

Subtitle support in  [Adaptive Video Streaming](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23906839206546-Adaptive-Video-Streaming)
 enhances video accessibility and inclusivity by allowing easy addition of multi-language subtitles. With the ability to upload, replace, download and delete.SRT & .VTT transcription files directly in the **Asset** **Detail** **View**, this feature gives you full control over the captioning capabilities for your Adaptive streaming Video Content. This feature helps make your content available to a broader audience, including non-native speakers and individuals with hearing impairments. With support for multiple languages and integrated video delivery [analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20094286365330-Delivery-Metrics-in-Advanced-Analytics)
, you can optimize content to reach international viewers, improve user engagement, and expand your viewer base. Additionally, automatic subtitle conversion and [AI transcription](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14713807155090-Speech-to-Text-for-Video-and-Audio-Assets)
 features minimize manual work, saving time for content managers.

How to Enable this feature?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25061882331922#h_01JNEA5H2971KPT7BEMGMSJ58C)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This feature is available for those who have [CX Omni Channel/ Dynamic Asset Transformation (DAT)](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18952775916562)
 enabled in your portal. 

How to Add Subtitles to Your Videos[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25061882331922#h_01JNEC5555Q2R3MBNA2EDZGEHQ)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Upload Your Own Subtitle File

1.  **Use a .VTT File**: The platform supports uploading subtitle files in the .VTT format, which is compatible with Adaptive Video Streaming.
2.  **Support for.SRT Files:** You can upload an.SRT subtitle file. The system will automatically convert it into a.VTT format during processing.

### Add Multiple Subtitles in Different Languages

You can upload subtitles in multiple languages for the same video, allowing you to reach a broader and more diverse audience.

### Use AI Speech to Text for Transcriptions

If you don’t have a .VTT file, you can use the [AI Speech to Text](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14713807155090-Speech-to-Text-for-Video-and-Audio-Assets)
 feature (available with the [AI package](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407741633426-AI-Search-Experience)
) to generate a transcription directly from your video. Once the transcription is generated, you’ll need to manually download it as a .VTT file and upload it to the platform.

How to Manage Subtitles[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/25061882331922#h_01JNEC5555D39QZMWX4D1CPJC8)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

To manage subtitles in Adaptive Video Streaming, follow these steps:

1.  Navigate to Asset Bank.
2.  Select the asset (**video**) you want to manage.
3.  Locate the **Subtitles** section in the Asset Detail View.
    *   New subtitles will be marked with a “**new**” tag for easy identification.
4.  The **Subtitles** dropdown menu is available for easy access to subtitle management options (upload, download, replace, delete).

    , download the transcription as a .VTT file and manually upload it to the Adaptive Video Streaming asset.
*   **File Format**: .VTT is the preferred file format for subtitles. If you upload an .SRT file, it will be automatically converted to .VTT during processing.
