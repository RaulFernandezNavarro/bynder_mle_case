# How To Use Bynder Express – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21575912878738

Summary[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21575912878738#h_01K58SHE9V4RHBAP5HEGN1CSMK)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bynder Express is a robust large-file sharing tool that can be accessed through a dedicated page or widget, enhancing visibility and usability within the portal. Users can share assets via email or a generated link. Assets can be uploaded into Bynder Express without being added to the asset bank, allowing seamless asset sharing both internally and externally. 

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21575912878738#h_01K58TJ9Z4VQQ8QNYV23XZC67G)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This feature/solution requires your Customer Success Contact to enable, but then individual permissions can be done by the Bynder Admin.

Once _**Bynder Express**_ is enabled users will need the _Access to Bynder Express_ permission to share files using Bynder Express. Users will need the _View personal transfer history_ permission to see their own Bynder Express history, and _View transfer history from others_ permission to view other users Bynder Express history. Users will need the _Manage Portal Settings_ permission to configure the Bynder Express settings for other users. 

Why?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21575912878738#h_01K58V29ZCGRBR2QFVRZ9VCSAM)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

With a more flexible interface, Bynder Express aligns with other file-sharing functionalities while offering an upgraded experience without needing third-party tools. Permissions for specific user groups can be tailored in Bynder Express, ensuring only authorized individuals can use this feature. Additionally, admins can customize various user options, such as limiting sharing methods (email or link) and specifying an expiration date for shared files. Bynder Express allows users to share files without needing to be logged in, or adding the files to the asset bank, allowing for a manageable way to share assets with anyone.

How?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21575912878738#h_01K58VKV7H5DZ00DBNNHJ5VE01)

### Configuring Bynder Express

1.  Navigate to **Portal Settings** > **Bynder** **Express**.                           ![Screenshot 2025-09-16 at 10.13.28.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241520146)
2.  Click the _Configure Bynder Express_ button. (This will only show to users with the correct permissions.) ![Configure Bynder Express.jpg](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241521554)
3.  Set a specific expiration period for assets shared via Bynder Express. Available options include: **1 Day, 1 Week, 1 Month,** and **Never**.
    
    *   At least _one_ expiry duration must always be enabled, as it dictates how long shared files will remain available for download.
        
    *   We recommend avoiding the "**Never**" expiry setting. Files that do not expire will cause them to take up storage space indefinitely. Once expired, the file will no longer count towards the portals storage allocation.
        
4.  Choose which file-sharing methods **(Email or Link)** to enable for Bynder Express.
    *   At least _one_ sharing method must be enabled to provide users with a way to share files.
5.  Once configured, click **Save** **Changes**. ![Screenshot 2025-02-27 at 1.23.25 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241522962)

### Sharing Assets In Bynder Express Via Email

1.  From your Bynder portal click the **S****ettings Wheel >**  **Bynder Express**.  ![Screenshot 2025-09-16 at 10.13.28.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241520146)
2.  Click **Share your files** and select the files you want to share.
    *   Users can add files later (before sending) by clicking **Add** **more files**.![Screenshot 2024-05-08 at 2.40.35 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507257043474)
3.  Select the **Email** tab.
4.  Enter the recipient's email address(es) (Up to eight).
5.  Optionally type a message for the recipient(s) in the text box.
6.  Select the expiration date from the dropdown options.  We recommend you _always_ select an expiration date.
7.   Select **Send.** Once the file has been successfully sent users will see a 100% icon. ![Screenshot 2024-05-08 at 2.53.15 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507257045906)
8.  The sender and the recipients will receive an email with a download link.

### Sharing Assets In Bynder Express Via Link

1.  From your Bynder portal click the **S****ettings Wheel >**  **Bynder Express**.  ![Screenshot 2025-09-16 at 10.13.28.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241520146)
2.  Click **Share your files** and select the files you want to share.
    *   Users can add files later (before sending) by clicking **Add** **more files**.![Screenshot 2024-05-08 at 2.40.35 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507257043474)
3.  Select the **Link** tab.
4.  Select the expiration date from the dropdown options.  We recommend you _always_ select an expiration date.
5.  Click **Send.** A download link will display once the transfer is complete. ![Screenshot 2024-05-08 at 3.30.25 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507257048466)
6.  Click the ![Screenshot 2024-05-08 at 3.31.35 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241528082) to copy the generated link or highlight and copy the URL.
7.  Share the link with others. Once they open the link a window will open and they can download the shared file. ![Screenshot 2024-05-08 at 3.32.41 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507257054866)

### Viewing Bynder Express Transfer History

*   Navigate to your Portal.
*   Select **Your UserName** \> **Transfer** **history,** then enjoy the following options:  
    *   View or sort the data:
        *   Hover over the **Recipients** **column** to view the complete list of recipients.
            
        *   Hover over the **Expires** **In** data to see the exact date and time of the expiration date.
            
    *   Monitor the status of the transfer: 
        *   **Red** means the transfer has expired.
        *   **Orange** represents the transfer expiring within 24 hours.
        *   **Green** indicates the files are still available for download.
*   Select the  ![linkIcon.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507257056274) to view and copy the generated Link for any available transfers. Once the transfer expires, the Link will be broken and unavailable to copy.
*   Select the ![downloadIcon.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241533586) to download the files for an available transfer.
*   Select the ![revokeIcon.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29507241535378)to revoke access to your transfer. The shared URL will stop working, and recipients can no longer download the shared file(s). The shared files will be removed from our server.

**Can a non-Bynder user access Bynder Express to share files if they have the URL?**  
No, users must be logged into the Bynder portal to share files via Bynder Express.

**How do I add Bynder Express to the header?**

If you would like to add Bynder Express to your Bynder portal menu header please contact your Customer Success Contact to enable this.

**Can I add Bynder Express to Custom Tabs?**  
Yes, the article how to for this is below!

**What if I want to revoke the URL that another user created?**

You can only revoke access to your transfers even if you are an admin. 

**What happens if I misplace the generated link?**

You can always view past-generated links in the Transfer History section of your portal. 

**Can I customize the expiration options of download links to be different than the default ones?**

As an admin you can decide which of the expiration options are visible to your users, i.e. _1 day, 1 week, 1 month_, or _Never_. It is not possible to add any other expiration options.
