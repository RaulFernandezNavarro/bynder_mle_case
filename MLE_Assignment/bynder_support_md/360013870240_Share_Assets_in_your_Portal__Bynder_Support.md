How to Create a Share Link for an Asset(s) [](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870240-Share-Assets-in-your-Portal#h_01HKB57KWBTVMKFFJTF44B4319)

Generate a shareable link to distribute to others, customizing access settings as needed. A shareable link can only be sent to other Bynder portal users. If you want to share an asset with an external user you can do so [via public link](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/22432310519954)
 or [create a Collection](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932639)
.

1.  Navigate to your Portal.
2.  Select the **Asset** section.
3.  [Filter or search](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360)
     for the asset(s) you want to share. 
    *   If you are unsure if the user you share the asset with has access to a certain asset, you can contact your portal admin.
4.  Click on the Asset you would like to share. ![Screenshot 2024-01-04 at 4.17.42 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/16180461075090)
5.   Use the toolbar to select the sharing options and click. ![Screenshot 2024-01-04 at 4.16.40 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/16180461076370) ![image-20250825-225944.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/29016836640786)
6.  Alternatively, you can share an asset through a direct link in the [asset detail view.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016051239)
     
7.  When you share one or more assets, a [Collections](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932639)
     is automatically created. You can name the Collection to help organize and track your shared assets.
