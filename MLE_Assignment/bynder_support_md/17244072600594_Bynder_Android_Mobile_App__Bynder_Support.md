# Bynder Android Mobile App – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17244072600594-Bynder-Android-Mobile-App

Access your Asset Bank on the go by using the Bynder Mobile App. You can search, filter, and view assets, add them to collections, favorite, and share them with others.

The App uses the permission set up in your portal and is available for Android and [iOS devices.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013871260)
