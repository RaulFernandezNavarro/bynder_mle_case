# Content Workflow Admin Training – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21723399671570-Content-Workflow-Admin-Training

In [this training session](https://academy.bynder.com/m/7cb13915bc28d019/original/Content-Workflow-admin-training-webinar.mp4)
, you'll discover how to optimize your content creation process with Content Workflow. 

In this training, we cover:

*   **Projects & Navigation:** Learn how to organize projects and navigate Content Workflow using real-life examples.
*   **Content Items & Templates:** Create structured content items and standardize processes with effective template management.
*   **Review & Feedback:** Understand the content review process, including how to manage annotations and track changes.
*   **Workflow Management:** Explore customizable workflows to improve content management efficiency.
*   **User Roles & Permissions:** Discover how to assign roles and manage permissions for better collaboration.
