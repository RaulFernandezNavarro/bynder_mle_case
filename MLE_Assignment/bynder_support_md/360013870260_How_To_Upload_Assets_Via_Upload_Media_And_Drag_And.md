# How To Upload Assets Via Upload Media And Drag And Drop – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870260

Summary[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870260#h_01JQR810JGC5WFV8JCKQBF9XMS)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can upload assets as a logged-in user by dragging and dropping files directly into your Portal or via the **Upload** **Media** button.

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870260#h_01JQR813E1FN483KD3YZJPKEJN)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Users with the **upload assets** or the **upload assets with mandatory approval** permissions can upload assets. Users with the _upload assets with mandatory approval_ permission cannot upload to the asset bank, their uploads will go directly to the _Waiting Room_ for approval. 

Why?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870260#h_01JQR817JWGA6W7NAH4B33CCQJ)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bynder makes uploading assets easy. You can upload up to 250 assets per upload, with a maximum file size of 50 GB. When uploading via the API neither dimension can exceed a pixel size of 25,000. 

The assets are imported with their embedded metadata (with a limit of 1000 characters per metafield); however, you can add additional metaproperties and tags during upload. Bynder recommends setting up metaproperty options before upload to easily manage the metadata options in your portal. 

With the ability to have filenames as titles, product numbers per asset, and asset numbering in names, uploading assets can cover a lions share of search-ability needs for your assets and save time for yourself and your enterprise.

How?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870260#h_01JQR8065WD3YCJNE50T4G3XWW)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to your Bynder Portal. ![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.01.24 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15157000052242)
2.  Select the **Upload** **Media** button located at the top right corner and select the files from your desktop _or_ navigate to the **Assets** tab to drag assets from your computer, and drop them directly into your Bynder portal.![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.04.22 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15157887613458)
3.  Enter the **title** for each asset. The character limit for the asset title field is 400 characters. You can manually input the title or **use the filenames as the title,** your Customer Success Contact can enable this feature for you.
    *   If you would like to automatically **use the filename as the title** contact Customer Support. 
    *   If enabled you can see the **original filename** in the info tab of your asset detail view.
    *   If you manually input a title and click **Make** **Titles** **Unique**, Bynder adds numbers to each asset in the upload batch. The user _must_ enable this manually, it cannot be automated by Bynder. ![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.07.48 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15156971384978)
    *   If you are uploading an asset with product numbers, we suggest you separate your product number with an underscore. IE. product\_1234.jpg. Without the separator (product1234.jpg), the product number will not be indexed and cannot be used for searches.
4.  Click on each tab to expand that metaproperty **o****ption**. You can make certain metaproperties required. An asterisk appears next to the required fields. ![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.49.58 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15157215041682)
5.  You can add additional metaproperties by clicking **Add** **Option**.![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.15.04 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15157000060562)
    *   If you select **No** **property**, the selection will clear.
    *   If you want to make edits to one asset make sure the asset is the only one highlighted.![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.19.40 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15157000062226)
6.  (Optional) Add tags to your assets to help classify and identify your assets.
    *   If a tag is displayed in a **white** **font** **color**, the tag applies to **all selected assets** in the upload or edit screen. If the tag is **grayed** **out**, it **doesn't apply** to all selected assets.![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.21.31 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15157000063122)
7.  Click **Store** **selected** **files (#)** to upload assets to your Asset Bank.![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 12.26.38 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15157880343442)

_Note: A preview for the asset will show even while metadata is being processed. Process time for metadata varies based on several factors, and is not expected to complete instantaneously._ 

_Note: If a recently uploaded asset does not appear immediately simply refresh your asset overview page._

**Why do some of my assets not have a preview after upload?**

Preview is only available on supported file types and sizes. See the article below in the _Related Articles_ section for a list of preview enabled file types.

**I have a lot of assets to upload, is this the best way?**

Bynder has a _Mass Uploader_ that would be helpful. Please see the below _Related Articles_ to learn how to upload using this feature.
