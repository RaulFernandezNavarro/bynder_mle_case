# View Internal and External Asset Usage in your Portal – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931759-View-Internal-and-External-Asset-Usage-in-your-Portal

You can monitor who viewed and downloaded an asset via the **Statistics** **tab** on the Asset detail page. In addition, you can measure asset performance and usage outside of the DAM.

Statistics are updated every day at approximately 5:15 AM UTC.

View Internal Asset Usage[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931759-View-Internal-and-External-Asset-Usage-in-your-Portal#h_01H90QNDMR1Y410MDWB158A8N0)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You’ll be able to see how many times an asset has been viewed and downloaded by users within your portal.

1.  Navigate to your Asset Bank.
2.  [Search](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931359)
     or [filter](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870120)
     for an asset.
3.  Click on the asset and select the **Statistics** tab**.**
4.  Here you can see the **Downloads** and **Views**.
5.  Hover over the name of the user to see how many times they viewed or downloaded the asset.
    *   Each file included in a download counts as a separate download. For example, if an image has two derivatives and the user clicks the Download all files, this will be counted as 3 downloads (1 original file + 2 derivatives).

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If your assets are marked as public or are using DAT, you can view their performance and usage outside of the DAM and leverage [Bynder's Content Delivery Network (CDN)](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013875140)
. 

1.  Navigate to your Asset Bank.
2.  [Search](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931359)
     or [filter](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870120)
     for an asset.
3.  Click on the asset and select the **Statistics** tab**.**
4.  Here you can see the following **External Traffic**
    *   All-time view: The number of times a DAT or public link asset is requested externally.
    *   Last 30-day views: A count of how many times the asset has been viewed externally over the past 30 days.
    *   Top transformations (if DAT is enabled): The top 4 DAT transformations.
    *   Top websites: The top 4 websites that the asset is displayed in.
        *   If a request does not include the referer header, the website will be counted under a hyphen "-" in the Top Websites section.
5.  Depending on your account type, clicking [**View** **Asset** **Report**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/10257152407058)
     may allow you to view a more detailed asset-level report.
