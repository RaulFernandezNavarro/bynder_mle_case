# Audit Requests in the Waiting Room – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870280-Waiting-Room

You can choose to have assets sent to the Waiting Room before they are sent to your Asset Bank. This helps manage the assets via [upload](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931479)
, [external uploader](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931459)
, [Asset Workflow](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932879)
, or [Integrations Hub](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/11400106439698)
 to ensure they meet brand standards and are approved. You can [control who has access](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21193687759890)
 to the Waiting Room and who can audit assets in the Waiting Room.

### Note

If you have [Antivirus Scan for Uploads to Waiting Room](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21193773236626)
 enabled, your portal assets will be flagged for viruses.

**Who can access the Waiting Room?**

Users with the following right(s) can fully access the Waiting Room and audit all assets pending approval:

*   [Audit upload requests in the Waiting Room](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433246503442-Permissions-in-Asset-Bank#:~:text=Audit%20upload%20requests%20%2D%20Allows%20users%20to%20audit%20upload)
    
*   Users who have [audit rights on the metaproperty access level](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013872840-Set-Permissions-on-a-Metaproperty-Options-Level#:~:text=Audit%20Upload%20%26%20Download%20Requests)
     can access the waiting room to audit the assets that are tagged with this metaproperty option (s)

How to Audit Requests in the Waiting Room[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870280-Waiting-Room#h_01HMYFA5CCXY8VNN2F473HHQC6)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to Portal > **Settings**\> **Waiting** **Room**.
2.  [Filter the assets](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870280-Waiting-Room#h_01HXFB4XB9WA0Z3NTGZ0SCJWNZ)
     to make it easier to navigate.
    *   Depending on how your portal is configured you can [customize the Waiting Room View](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21193958826130)
         for your users.
3.  Select the request you would like to audit.
    
    ### Tip
    
    You can open an asset to see a bigger preview of the asset and check the metadata if it is available.
    
4.  Check the **Request info** in the left sidebar.
    
5.  Select the media you would like to audit and click **Approve request** to approve the request or click **Deny request** to reject the request.
    
    *   \[Optional\] Click **Approve** or **Deny all** to approve or reject all assets.
        
    *   \[Optional\] Permanently delete assets by selecting them and clicking the ![delete-icon.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10641635227154) icon.
        
        ### Caution
        
        If you deny a request, users cannot request it again. To approve a previously denied request, navigate to **Denied Requests** > select the asset > **Approve**.
        
    

###                     Note

                         Similar to [batch editing](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870340-Edit-Assets-in-Asset-Bank)
, the modification of metaproperties for assets that have been                                        approved in the waiting room does not automatically reveal dependencies.

How to Filter Assets in the Waiting Room[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870280-Waiting-Room#h_01HXFB4XB9WA0Z3NTGZ0SCJWNZ)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to ![settings_menu.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10641647406226)
2.  Click on the **Waiting room**
    
    ![WAITING_ROOM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10641647421586)
    
3.  Select the filters in the left sidebar to find the assets you're looking for. The applied filters can be viewed in the top bar of the Waiting Room.
    
    ![WAITING_ROOM_FILTER_PENDING_REQUESTS_ETC.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10641647447826)
    
4.  Once you are done filtering the requests, you can begin approving.

**Hi, how can I increase the approval limit on the waiting room? My approval limit is currently set to 20 assets per approval.**

[Contact Customer Support](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013871840)
 if you want to increase this limit. The maximum number of assets is 50.
