# How To Embed Content In Guides And Landing Pages – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802

Summary[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802#h_01JTFTA7X1SHERK85Z5MCQC5X2)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Embedding widgets in Guides and Landing Pages makes it simple to incorporate multimedia content from Vimeo, YouTube, or Figma, even if the content is not part of your DAM library.

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802#h_01JTFTGZFAGXHQA9YPY8X3SS75)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Users with the _Creator_ or _Manager_ permission can create or customize Guide or Landing Page content.

Why?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802#h_01JTFTM8QKTN88R1GA3ZZM2GE5)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bynder knows that not all the content you want to use will live in the DAM. This means that you need to be able to seamlessly embed content into your _Guides_ or _Landing Pages._ After pasting the link your content will be displayed. Using an embed link also ensures that updates made to the content are reflected in real-time within Bynder. 

How?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802#01J9W52MXZA5GRYVEXSJ8GGAAY)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Adding Vimeo Embeds

1.  Select the ![Screenshot 2024-11-18 at 3.54.53 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/22738106589202) button -> **Embed** -> select **Vimeo**.
2.  Enter the **URL** in the right-hand widget settings bar.
3.  Enable **Show details** for the title of the video to appear in the header of the video.

(Optional) Enable **Autoplay** and **Loop** options.

*   *   Autoplay videos are muted

![Screenshot 2024-11-18 at 4.00.34 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/22738106589842)[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802#01JD0H91NQZHDMKM6TBWPAWHEY)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Adding Youtube Embeds

1.  Select the ![Screenshot 2024-11-18 at 3.54.53 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/22738106589202)button -> **Embed** -> **YouTube**.
2.  Enter the **YouTube** **URL** in the right-hand widget settings bar.
3.  Enable the **Controls option** so that users can play, pause, and control the volume of the video. 

(Optional)Enable **Autoplay** and **Loop** options.

*   *   Autoplay videos are muted.

![Screenshot 2024-11-18 at 3.58.46 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26486839653778)[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802#01JD0GR6MAYHRYMT3AH02K8BBS)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Adding Figma Embeds

1.  Select the ![Screenshot 2024-11-18 at 3.54.53 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/22738106589202)button -> **Embed** -> **Figma.**
2.  Enter the **Figma** **URL** in the right-hand widget settings bar.
    
3.  **Controls:** select toggle to show/ hide the visibility of controls (zoom in/out) in the Figma file.
4.  **Footer:** select toggle to show/hide the visibility of the footer (file title and last edited information).
5.  **Theme:** Choose a theme for your design view:  
    *   **System**: Matches your system's theme
    *   **Light**: Default theme
    *   **Dark**: Ideal for reducing eye strain and blending Figma files with darker backgrounds.

**What kind of Figma files are supported?**

Figma design files are currently supported.

**What happens if the video embedded from Youtube or Vimeo is removed?**

The content will no longer be available within Bynder, and the URL should be replaced or removed.
