# GDPR in Bynder – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936059-GDPR-in-Bynder

We are happy to announce that Bynder complies with the new European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Find out below how we respect your privacy in your Bynder portal and what you can do to modify your user data or to have it permanently removed.

What personally identifiable information does Bynder collect?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936059-GDPR-in-Bynder#h_01JKSY0X2TD8V5GXN2J7Y6WX8N)
