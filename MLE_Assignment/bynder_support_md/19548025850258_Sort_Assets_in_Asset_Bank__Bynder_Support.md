# Sort Assets in Asset Bank – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/19548025850258-Sort-Assets-in-Asset-Bank

By default, assets in the [Asset](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016051239)
 Library are sorted by **Date** **Published** in descending order.

You can combine the sorting options with ascending or descending order. For instance, assets can be sorted from the most recent to the least recent, or alphabetically from A-Z or Z-A. Use the arrow icon to toggle between ascending and descending sorting.

To help navigate through your assets you have several options for sorting assets:

*   **Order by Date Added:** View assets based on when they were added to the Assets Bank.
*   **Order by Date Published**: Sort assets by their publication date or the time they were viewed. Future publication dates are taken into account and displayed accordingly.
*   **Order by Date Modified:** Arrange assets by the date they were last modified.
*   **Order by Name:** Sort assets alphabetically by their filename.

![update_order_by_date_etc__.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/19547983231250)

### How to enable this feature in your portal?

If you prefer to change the default view to **Date** **Added** or in **ascending** **order**, please contact your Customer Success Contact.
