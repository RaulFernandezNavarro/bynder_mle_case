### Asset Uploader

Import assets to Bynder from publicly available URLs

Select to compare  Compare

The Gournay Consulting Asset Uploader enables users to batch-upload digital assets directly into Bynder by simply listing public URLs in an Excel spreadsheet. Users can map metadata fields—such as filename and custom attributes—within the same file to automatically update asset information upon upload. This streamlines bulk imports, eliminates manual dragging and dropping, and reduces errors by validating links before transfer. By leveraging this connector, teams save time, maintain data consistency, and simplify asset onboarding workflows. Whether migrating existing resources or updating metadata for extensive collections, the Gournay Consulting Asset Uploader provides a fast, reliable, and user-friendly bridge between a spreadsheet-based organization and Bynder's powerful DAM capabilities.

### Seismic

Automatic content synchronization between Bynder and Seismic.

Select to compare  Compare

This integration enables users to automatically synchronize designated content from the Bynder DAM to the Seismic Library, eliminating the need for manual distribution of content from Bynder to Seismic. It ensures that content from Bynder is kept up to date in Seismic, and content that is deleted or archived in Bynder is automatically deleted or unpublished in Seismic, enhancing compliance and reducing manual effort. It also allows users to configure synchronization of content to one or more TeamSites, streamlining content organization and distribution.
