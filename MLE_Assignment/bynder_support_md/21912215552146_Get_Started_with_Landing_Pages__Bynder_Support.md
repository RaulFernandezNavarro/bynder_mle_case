# Get Started with Landing Pages – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21912215552146-Get-Started-with-Landing-Pages

The [Landing Page](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21908454150802)
 solution in the [CX User Community](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21861208799634)
 lets you create personalized, visually engaging experiences for your audience. With this flexible tool, you can build content hubs, playbooks, and training materials for [product launches](https://brand-experiences.bynder.com/guidelines/guide/0cd41855-4ff7-45f0-b361-cbc713b9943e/page/e62d4ef9-49da-4b21-be17-a06af1430c55)
, [GTM,](https://vimgroup-stylr.getbynder.com/guidelines/guide/b651759e-5e6f-4748-9475-3f9a3e997e73)
 [training, enablement](https://vimgroup-stylr.getbynder.com/guidelines/guide/48b8dc33-4aa3-43d3-bcb1-4257ff5a1299)
 or direct users to specific DAM content.

**Who can create and customize content in guide and landing pages?**

### Note

Use Landing Pages to create a [fully customized, self-serviceable portal homepage](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/23901110488210)
 that enhances user engagement and drives portal adoption.

Get Started with Landing Pages[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21912215552146-Get-Started-with-Landing-Pages#h_01JA8W4RDBSRH7R3VQ2W4WGFZY)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  [Create a Landing Page](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21908795341714)
    
2.  Add [Media Widgets](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909840491666)
     and [text or additional customizations](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909974085778)
    . 
    *   [Embed video content](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909976214802)
         hosted on YouTube or Vimeo.
3.  [Edit the layout](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21911971836050)
     (such as columns, margins, and padding) to personalize your landing page's structure.
4.  Enhance your landing page by adding a [customized background](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21911866201106)
     to each section and adjusting the opacity or applying a filter to your background to improve the readability of overlaying text and media.
5.  Maintain consistency with your brand by applying a [color palette](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21911813533074)
     to your page.
6.  Once you are ready you can [Publish and Share Your Landing Pages.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21912018694418)
    *   Depending on the features in your CX for User Community [package, you can share content with specific audiences](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21909413766546)
        .
