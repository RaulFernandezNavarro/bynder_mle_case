# iFrame Support – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013873280-iFrame-Support

Bynder does not support using iFrame as it can sometimes not be secure. Instead, we recommend you use [embed code](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870040)
 or [public URLs.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870200)
 

NOTE: iFrames are supported in Bynder [Bynder Guidelines 1.0](https://support.bynder.com/hc/articles/360013871980#UUID-76402b2a-60f3-be84-a9a3-0a8a82c98015)
.
