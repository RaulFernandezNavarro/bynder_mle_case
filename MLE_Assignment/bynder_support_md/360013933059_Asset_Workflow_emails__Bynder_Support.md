# Asset Workflow emails – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013933059-Asset-Workflow-emails

Refer to the information to learn more which emails are sent from Asset Workflows, when they are sent and who they are sent to. Note that you will never receive e-mails about your own actions, or double emails about the same actions.

Job: New

This notification is also sent when a job is created using the API.

*   When: after creating a job based on a preset 
    
*   Who: the responsibles of the active stage of the job
    

Job: Finish

This notification is also sent when a job has been created using the API.

*   When: after finishing a job
    
*   Who: the responsibles of the active stage of the job and the accountable of the job
    

Message: New

*   When: after adding a message to a job/asset
    
*   Who (job): the responsible users of the active stage of the job, the accountable of the job and the job creator
    
    ### Note
    
    When it's a private message, it's only sent to the users the private message is shared with.
    
*   Who (asset) the owner(s) of the asset and its versions
    

Message: Reply

*   When: after someone adds a reply to a message
    
*   Who: the user who wrote the original message that is being replied to
    

Message: Reply New

*   When: after someone adds a reply to a message
    
*   Who: the users who replied to the original message before
    

Message: Mention

*   When: after someone mentions you in a message
    
*   Who: the tagged users
    

Order: New

*   When: when a new order is placed, a order summary is sent
    
*   Who: the email address which is entered in the preset, after selecting **Send PDF via email**
    

Order: Update

*   When: when an update for a placed order is received (placed, received, production started, shipped)
    
*   Who: the user who placed the order
    

Stage: Next

This notification is also sent when a job is created using the API.

*   When: after sending a job to a next stage
    
*   Who: the responsibles of the current stage, the responsibles of the stage the job is being sent to and the accountable of the job
    

Stage: Previous

This notification is also sent when a job is created using the API.

*   When: after sending a job to a previous stage
    
*   Who: the responsibles of the current stage, the responsibles of the stage the job is being sent to and the accountable of the job
    

Stage: Responsible Changed

*   When: after changing the responsible(s) of a stage
    
*   Who: the old responsibles of the stage, the new responsibles of the stage and the accountable of the job
    

Stage: User Invite

*   When: after inviting a new user when progressing the job to a next stage (this required the options **Choose responsible** and **Allow user invite** options to be enabled in the preset the job is based on)
    
*   Who: the invited user
    

Task: Completed

*   When: after completing a task
    
*   Who: the creator of the task and the responsibles of the task (if someone other than the assigned user completes the task)
    

Task: Responsible Assigned

*   When: after assigning the responsible(s) of a task
    
*   Who: the responsible(s) of the task
    

Task: Responsible Changed

*   When: after changing the responsible(s) of a task
    
*   Who: the new responsible(s) of the task
