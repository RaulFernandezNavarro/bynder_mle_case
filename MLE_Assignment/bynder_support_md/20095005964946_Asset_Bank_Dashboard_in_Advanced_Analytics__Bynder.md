# Asset Bank Dashboard in Advanced Analytics – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20095005964946-Asset-Bank-Dashboard-in-Advanced-Analytics

The Asset Bank Dashboard is located in [Advanced Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14829008886930)
. It provides a comprehensive overview of asset performance and user activity. It helps you monitor upload and download trends, identify top-performing content, and understand user engagement.

**Who can view analytics reports?**

Users with the following permissions can access and download reports:

*   [**View advanced reporting**](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433246503442-Permissions-in-Asset-Bank#:~:text=View%20advanced%20reporting%20%2D%20Allows%20users%20to%20access%20Bynder%20Analytics.)

This dashboard allows you to explore downloads and views, breaking them down across taxonomy and users. This provides insights into content performance beyond just the top 10 assets.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/20095028825490)

### Key Questions

*   How many unique assets are being downloaded?
*   How is a specific asset (e.g., asset XYZ) performing?
*   Who’s downloading?
*   Who’s not downloading?
*   What groups of content are performing best/worst?

Glossary [](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20095005964946-Asset-Bank-Dashboard-in-Advanced-Analytics#h_01J2HGGFF0F16QXXDMZ6A9V5M7)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Unique Download:** The number of unique assets downloaded. For example, if a user downloads the same asset twice, it is counted as one unique download.

Uploads[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20095005964946-Asset-Bank-Dashboard-in-Advanced-Analytics#h_01J2HGGFF0DWFN8SARMPC025AJ)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Upload Dashboard focuses on asset uploads and deletions, categorized by taxonomy and users. This section also reveals who has recently deleted assets. The upload analytics may show a higher number than if searching the asset bank uploads by date because the analytics metric will include _waiting room_ assets. 

### Key Questions

*   How much content am I uploading?
*   Who’s uploading the most content?
*   Who’s uploading the best-performing content, and can I share their best practices?

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/20095005954450)Embargoes[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20095005964946-Asset-Bank-Dashboard-in-Advanced-Analytics#h_01J2HGGFF0VFB0444AM4PQ07W4)

Embargo Dashboard delves into assets scheduled to archive, watermark, or have their limits removed. It offers an overview of metaproperties and identifies which are still being downloaded.

### ![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/20095005954834)Key Questions

*   What assets are scheduled to be archived?
*   What assets are scheduled to watermark?
*   What assets are scheduled to have their limit removed?
*   Are these assets still being downloaded, and should I consider not archiving them?
*   Who has downloaded assets recently that will be archived so I can contact them not to use them?

Detailed Analytics[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20095005964946-Asset-Bank-Dashboard-in-Advanced-Analytics#h_01J2HGGFF0KA8KG0DBQY93Z1E3)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Detailed Analytics provides the most granular data available, allowing for deeper analysis or [export to Excel](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14465131283218-How-to-View-and-Download-Analytics-Reports)
 for further examination.
