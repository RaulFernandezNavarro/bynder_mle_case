### DAM

#### [Targeted Homepage](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/28990876159634-How-To-Create-A-Targeted-Homepage)

Targeted homepages improve engagement by ensuring every user sees specific content for them, while also giving admins greater control over how content is shared and managed across audiences. Whether by different region, language, brands or user roles, you can deliver a personalized DAM Homepage content experience that resonates.

####   
Introducing Okta And Azure SCIM Functionality In Bynder

Bynder now supports SCIM functionality, including creating, updating and deactivating user accounts with Okta. SCIM, or System for Cross-domain Identity Management, is an open standard designed to automate the process of user identity management and provisioning across different cloud-based services and applications.   
[How To Enable And Use Azure SCIM With Bynder](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/29068712565010-How-To-Enable-And-Use-Azure-SCIM-With-Bynder)
  
[How To Enable And Use Okta SCIM With Bynder](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/27929897102866-How-To-Enable-And-Use-Okta-SCIM-With-Bynder)

### CX Omnichannel

#### [Prevent Content Leaks](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/29142467691922-How-To-Enable-And-Use-Token-Based-Public-Link-Access)

With token-based authentication, give teams working in pre-production environments the ability to see public assets, while restricting visibility for others. 

### Analytics

#### [Better Understand Your DAM Taxonomy](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/29141834794386-Understanding-The-Taxonomy-Dashboards)

With the new Taxonomy report, you can self-serve to audit, clean, and manage your DAM metadata. Get a complete view of your assets and instantly take control of your DAM metadata with self-service reporting.

#### [Get A Full Picture Of Your DAM Assets](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/29141589258130-Understanding-The-Asset-Report-Dashboard)

Instantly get a full, exportable list of all your DAM assets. Bypassing the need for manual reports from our Platform Services team, you can now run your own audits and migrations at any time.
