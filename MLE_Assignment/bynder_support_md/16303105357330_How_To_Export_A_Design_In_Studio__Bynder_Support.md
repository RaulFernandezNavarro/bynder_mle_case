Once you are done with a design in Studio, you can export your variation(s) to either the Waiting Room to be added to the Asset Bank, or download the variation(s) to the users device.

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/16303105357330-How-To-Export-A-Design-In-Studio#h_01JRFSY8EYX236F09WWR5ZKTVK)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This feature/solution requires your Customer Success Contact to enable, but then individual permissions can be done by the Bynder Admin.

Once Studio is enabled this feature is available to all users.

Why?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/16303105357330-How-To-Export-A-Design-In-Studio#h_01JRFT6653MV5YR0WX0AJ66SVN)

Studio users need to be able to use the creations made in Studio within Bynder, or other external tools. 

How?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/16303105357330-How-To-Export-A-Design-In-Studio#h_01JRFT7XH9PCNMSDJJWWWNXC61)

### Export A Studio Design As A PDF

1.  **Finalize your design** in Studio.
    
2.  Click the **Export** button.
    
3.  **Choose the pages** you wish to export.
    
4.  From the Format dropdown menu, **select the PDF** option.
    
5.  Click Download or Send-to-waiting-room, and the PDF will be generated and downloaded.
    

_Note: PDF files are "flat" for graphics, but text should be able to be derived._ 

### Export A Studio Design As A ZIP File

1.  Navigate to Studio.
2.  Select the design you would like to export. 
3.  You can either:
    *   Hover over a design and click the ![Screenshot 2024-01-10 at 8.18.50 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/16303243318546) button -> Select **Export![Screenshot 2024-01-10 at 8.20.28 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/16303197507090)**
    *    Or, open up your design, and click Export in the top right corner**. ![exPorT-But0n.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/25950050283666)** 
4.  The export pop-up will open, and from here users can select the variation(s)/pages. Press the **Change Selection** button to open a more detailed view of the variation(s)/pages, to give higher visibility for the export. Once you press **save selection** you will be brought back to the original pop-up window. 
5.   Choose where you would like the variation(s) to go:
    *   **Waiting Room:** This will go to the Waiting Room, where it can be approved and added to the DAM.
    *   **Download:** This will download to the users device.![download presets.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/25950021335826)
6.  Decide what file format is best for the download/export. ![Format one.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/25950021353746)
7.  Press the **download (#) file** button. ![Modal-Not-popup.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/25950050294162)
8.  The download will begin, and a pop-up window will appear. This pop-up will have a clickable _View in Activity_ section that brings users to the _Activity Log_.

![see this is nice.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/25950021357714)

### Activity Log

You can view the status of your exports in the **Activity log.** Here, you can filter by **Type,** or **Status.** You can also **delete** an export in the Activity Log.
