-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The CX for User Community Advanced Analytics Dashboard provides in-depth insights into content performance, audience engagement, and distribution. It categorizes content into Guides and Landing Pages for detailed analysis, enabling content creators to track user views, identify high-performing or underperforming content, and gain a deeper understanding of their audience. The dashboard also tracks external traffic and offers device-specific data to optimize content. The dashboard helps refine content strategies, improve audience retention, and encourage content creation by answering key questions about who engages with content and how it is accessed.

### Guide and Landing Page Views 

*   Total number of views across all Guides and Landing Pages.

### Unique Visitors 

*   The number of distinct users engaging with your content.

### Performance Insights 

*   Identifies high-performing content. 
*   Highlights underperforming content for improvement opportunities.

### Audience Groups and Profiles

*   Provides demographic and behavioral data on users interacting with content.
*   Tracks the types of devices used for access (desktop, mobile, tablet).

### External Sharing Detection

*   Monitors when and how content is accessed outside the platform.
