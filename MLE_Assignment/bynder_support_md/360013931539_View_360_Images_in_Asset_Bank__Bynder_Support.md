# View 360° Images in Asset Bank – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931539-View-360-Images-in-Asset-Bank

360° images provide an immersive experience, making you feel like you are at the location where the photo was captured. You can explore the surroundings by interacting with the image using our 360° Image Viewer, allowing you to look around by clicking and dragging your mouse.

How to Upload 360° Assets[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931539-View-360-Images-in-Asset-Bank#h_01HXFZE1E0330N8S8RZ9BSYKBN)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We support the following file format **JPEG** with a 2:1 aspect ratio. 

How to View 360° Assets[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931539-View-360-Images-in-Asset-Bank#h_01J0BR3X5PEQ6X68YBK34AC50R)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Select the asset that you want to open. 
    
2.  Click ![360_degrees.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10641647864850) located in the left bottom of the preview to enable the 360° Image Viewer.
    
3.  Click and drag to move through the image.
    *   If you do not see the ![360_degrees.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/10641647864850) icon that indicates the asset does not support 360° View.

FAQ[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931539-View-360-Images-in-Asset-Bank#h_01HXFZFG3BAH067XD1Q4K1J4E2)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**What if I have another 360° file format that is not a JPEG?**

Contact Customer Support who can assist with this request.
