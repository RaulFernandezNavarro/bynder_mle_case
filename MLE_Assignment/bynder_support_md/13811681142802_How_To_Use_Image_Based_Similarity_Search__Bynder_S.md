# How To Use Image Based Similarity Search – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/13811681142802

Summary[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/13811681142802#h_01JW5V8FKQ6RXGJMEPXD9HE0A6)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bynder's _Similarity Search_ allows users to easily find similar images within the Asset Bank, without needing to rely on metadata. This can be helpful if you’re looking for images of a particular product or images that may have a similar look and feel to group together in a collection.  

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/13811681142802#h_01JW5V8NG315HBYK1D6MAARNNS)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This feature/solution can only be enabled by your Customer Success Contact. 

Once the feature is enabled all users will have access to _Similarity Search_.

_Note: Any permission restrictions set on an image asset will be honored and will affect if an image asset is retrievable using this feature._

Why?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/13811681142802#h_01JW5VD17PCWGMNHVJDZPET0FF)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Keeping the Asset Bank clean and up-to-date is even easier with Bynder's _Similarity Search_. This allows Bynder users to find similar images and manage them as needed. Combing through a full Asset Bank can take an exceptional amount of time, but with _Similarity Search_ users can find exactly what images they are looking for with the click of a button. 

How?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/13811681142802#h_01HAN6B8PH4K1HZTENX13VC5H0)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  1.  Navigate to the **Asset Bank**.
    2.  Hover over an image and click **Show similar,** or users can view images in the Asset Detail View and by clicking the **show similar** button. Results are based on the asset selected. ![Screenshot 2024-04-08 at 1.15.25 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/18187685377938)  
        *   Users can select multiple images and click the ![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 2.32.01 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15158639357202) button in the toolbar. The results are then based on the similarity of _all_ of the selected images. Users can see up to 500 image results.  ![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 2.31.35 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15158639361042)  Images will be sorted based on _visual_ similarity to the selected asset(s).
            *   To remove _Similarity Search_, click **Clear filters** or the **X** next to **Filtering on Visual similarity**.![Screenshot 2023-11-17 at 2.26.59 PM.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/15158619215762)

_Note:There is a minimum image requirement of 128 X 128 pixel._

FAQs[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/13811681142802#h_01JW5W54DEXEGEACXKKVVM0JW7)

**Can I use _Similarity Search_ on videos?**

No, this is used for images at this time.
