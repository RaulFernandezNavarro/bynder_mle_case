# How To Create Collection Groups – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26007812298642-How-To-Create-Collection-Groups

Summary[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26007812298642-How-To-Create-Collection-Groups#h_01JRQFCEARK12TH6M1XGZE1FDK)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Collection Groups empower users to categorize their collections in a meaningful way and share them with ease with the correct stakeholders.

Who?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26007812298642-How-To-Create-Collection-Groups#h_01JRQFEECPD0Z1456M0XY9SMB9)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This feature/solution is enable-able by a Bynder Admin.

Users with the _Create a Collection_ permission can create their own _Collections,_ and therefore create Collection Groups. In order to share Collection Groups within the portal the user must have the _Manage Collection Groups_ permission. _Manage Collection Groups_ allows the user to share Collection Groups they have access to, even if they do not own them. The collections in the group will follow the visibility set up for the collection, users must share the collection if they wish for them to be seen within the group. Users must have a collection shared with them or own the collection in order to add them to the group. 

_Note: Collection Groups cannot be shared publicly._

Why?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26007812298642-How-To-Create-Collection-Groups#h_01JRQG8PH0SFY26TJEVFK6YZQT)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Collections are a valuable way to categorize your assets, and using groups allows you to categorize your collections. Grouping collections enables users to share more efficiently with necessary stakeholders, find relevant collections easily, and ensure easier discoverability.

How?[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26007812298642-How-To-Create-Collection-Groups#h_01JRQGQV5EAE41SH3RVQYQMQ5R)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1\. Navigate to **Collections** in your portal.

2\. From your left-side menu select **Groups.**

![Collectiongroups.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007812296082)

3\. This will open your _Groups_ menu. If you have never created a group you will have two _New Group_ button options. If you have already created groups then you will only see the _New Group_ button in the top left of your screen. Click **New Group.**

![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.09.58.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007812296722)

4\. A _Create new group_ pop-up will appear asking for you to **N****ame** the group and _optionally_ describe the group. This will enable the _Continue_ button. Click **Continue**.

![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.17.46.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007812296978)

5\. Navigate to _My collections_ from the left-hand side menu in _Collections_. 

![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.24.44.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007786296338)

6\. Hover over the collection you wish to add to a group, press the _three dots_ button. Click **Add to group.** 

![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.24.52.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007812297618)

7\. An _Add to group_ pop-up will open, here you can select an existing group for the collection, or you can create a new group. Click **Add.**

![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.25.12.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007786296594)

8\. _(Optional)_The collection has now been added to the specified group. You can hover over the collection, select the _three dots_ button, and open the dropdown. From here you can remove the collection from the group. Click **Remove from group.**

**![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.25.37.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007812298002)**

9._(Optional)_ Click the ![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.45.14.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007812298130)button to copy the link to share the group. The collection permissions of the user you are sharing with will be honored. 

_Note: To find groups you can use the ![Screenshot 2025-04-13 at 14.49.20.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/26007812298514)bar from within the Group overview. Groups cannot be searched anywhere else._

FAQs[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/26007812298642-How-To-Create-Collection-Groups#h_01JRQKAXBR7M5ETZG5X0H6PZGS)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Why can't I share a group, but I can create a collection?**

You need the _Manage Collection Groups_ permission to share a group. If you do not have this permission contact your Bynder Admin.

**Can I add a collection to more than one group?**

Yes, you can add the collection to multiple groups to fit your needs.

**Is there a way to limit the view of Collections Groups for users with the Manage Groups permission?  
**No, if you have the Manage Groups permission you will see all groups in the portal.

**Is it possible to disable "Whole Portal"?**  
No.
