# Bynder iOS Mobile App – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013871260-Bynder-iOS-Mobile-App

Access your Asset Bank on the go by using the Bynder Mobile App. You can search, filter, and view assets, add them to collections, favorite, and share them with others. If you want to access your assets and do not have access to service or WiFi, you can view assets in [Offline Mode](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013871260-Bynder-iOS-Mobile-App#h_01HQBG2YA0RPNA59BFWWZ66Y5S)
.

The App uses the permission set up in your portal and is available for iOS and [Android](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17244072600594)
 devices.
