# Reset your Password – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013934219

You can reset your Bynder password following these steps.

If your account uses [Single Sign On,](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936419)
 you cannot reset your Bynder password using the method below. Instead, you need to reset your personal SSO credentials.

How to Reset Your Password[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013934219#h_01HQBN8B7H9GTKR16Z2CQY5ZS8)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Navigate to your Bynder portal. 
2.  On the login page, click on **Lost password**.
3.  If you cannot see the hyperlink, it may be hidden in the hyperlink under the SSO login button.![lost-passoword.png](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/8873827928722)
4.  Click the hyperlink underneath the login button to display the standard login fields and your lost password hyperlink.
5.   Enter your email address.
6.  Select **Send instructions**.
7.  You will receive a notification sent to your email address.
    *   The notification has a link you need to click to reset your password.
8.  Please enter your new password and re-enter it.
    *   Check out [Bynder's Password Policy.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013936319)
        
9.  Save changes. After saving the reset password, you will successfully log into your Bynder portal.
