# DAM Audit Checklist for Quarterly Maintenance – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21214187532562-DAM-Audit-Checklist-for-Quarterly-Maintenance

This is a quick guide to auditing your Bynder DAM and provides a streamlined approach for a fast, focused review of your digital assets. It’s ideal for routine maintenance, ensuring asset integrity, and optimizing performance between more [comprehensive annual audits](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21213841926418)
.

Set Clear Objectives[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21214187532562-DAM-Audit-Checklist-for-Quarterly-Maintenance#h_01J74997EJ6JX3RG5W1QAD6G5T)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Why are you auditing your assets (e.g., compliance, organization, usage analysis)?

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Identify stakeholders and involve team members who manage, use, or are responsible for assets.

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Develop a review plan with your internal stakeholders on what aligns with brand initiatives or if assets include out-of-date materials.

Prepare for Audit[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21214187532562-DAM-Audit-Checklist-for-Quarterly-Maintenance#h_01J74997EJN9QN0H1MF32MWXWD)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Promote the DAM audit project and communicate with stakeholders. 

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Create a "**snapshot**" of the current DAM to compare growth and changes next year. Use portal usage and [analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/20047736186770-Practical-Tips-for-Analyzing-Reports-in-Analytics?_gl=1%2Add0fsm%2A_up%2AMQ..%2A_ga%2AMzY0MTgwNTMwLjE3MjExNjI5NTg.%2A_ga_W84HKH8FKC%2AMTcyMTE2Mjk1Ny4xLjAuMTcyMTE2Mjk1Ny4wLjAuMA..)
 to see the success in metrics.

Assess Assets [](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21214187532562-DAM-Audit-Checklist-for-Quarterly-Maintenance#h_01J74997EJ2TDJ9191841BHWNF)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Use Bynder [Analytics](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932579)
 to review asset performance. Use the information to see what assets have not been viewed or downloaded, and set a threshold with your stakeholders (in the last year, two years, etc.). 

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Determine if you need to [archive](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931059-Archive-Assets)
 or [delete](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021030779-Delete-Assets-from-Asset-Bank)
 outdated assets. 

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Check asset compliance and verify that all assets comply with licensing agreements.

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Ensure assets adhere to your company's Brand Guidelines and do not contain out-of-date information.

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)(If enabled) Use [Bynder AI](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407741633426-AI-Search-Experience)
 to identify duplicate assets quickly.

Review Metaproperties[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21214187532562-DAM-Audit-Checklist-for-Quarterly-Maintenance#h_01J74997EJMV9XJ236P78PHEFK)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Clean up the [metaproperties](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6190351879186-Create-and-Edit-Metaproperties-and-Metaproperty-Options)
 list and remove unused taxonomy options.

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Ensure that assets are properly marked with the correct [metaproperty](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/19460749107218-Edit-and-Delete-Metaproperties)
 information and related [tags](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/19505690386962-Manually-Add-Tags-to-Existing-Assets?_gl=1*8xdsh5*_up*MQ..*_ga*MTY0MzI0NjIwLjE3MjExNzY0OTI.*_ga_W84HKH8FKC*MTcyMTE3NjQ5MS4xLjAuMTcyMTE3NjQ5MS4wLjAuMA..)
 are cleaned up and updated.

User Permissions[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21214187532562-DAM-Audit-Checklist-for-Quarterly-Maintenance#h_01J74997EJA1M0HHGVGQREAHR0)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Ensure users have appropriate access levels. Review the [permission profiles](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/10309197472914-Permission-Management)
 and decide if you need additional profiles.

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Adjust roles and permissions as necessary if there have been any company changes.

Plan for the Future[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21214187532562-DAM-Audit-Checklist-for-Quarterly-Maintenance#h_01J74997EJ5VV0DP0FZNWEFHMY)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Schedule regular audits to maintain asset quality.

![unchecked](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21214163550994)Gather feedback from users to improve asset management practices.
