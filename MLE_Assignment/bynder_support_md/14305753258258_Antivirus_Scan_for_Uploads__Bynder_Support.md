# Antivirus Scan for Uploads – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14305753258258-Antivirus-Scan-for-Uploads

Bynder can scan assets uploaded via the [External Uploader](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931459)
 or [Waiting Room](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/Waiting%20Room)
 and flag them as infected. The antivirus solution scans assets for viruses and malware before they enter the Waiting Room and quarantines those that might be malicious. Any assets flagged as malicious or unscanned due to the file size (4GB or larger) will appear in the Quarantine section of your portal.

Once an asset (under 4GB) is uploaded via the [External Uploader](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931459-Upload-Assets-as-an-External-User-External-Uploader-)
 or to the [Waiting Room](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870280-Waiting-Room)
, it is scanned and sent to Quarantine if infected. Assets over 4 GB are automatically added to the Quarantine.

This feature/solution requires your Customer Success Contact to enable, but then individual permissions can be done by the Bynder Admin.

**Who can audit assets in the quarantine**

Users with the following permission can audit assets in quarantine:

*   Audit quarantined assets

Admin Tip: We recommend that the portal admin assigns a user, such as someone from IT or Infosec, to be the **Q****uarantine** **Admin** with the above permission.

How the Antivirus Scan for Waiting Room Works[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14305753258258-Antivirus-Scan-for-Uploads#h_01J71XNBFZX9TDRB3YNVEKQ7P0)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  [Upload an asset to the Waiting Room.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931479)
    
2.  When a file is uploaded to the Waiting Room, it does not immediately become available in your asset library. The antivirus scan is triggered automatically upon upload.
3.  Bynder's antivirus software scans the file for potential threats, such as malware, viruses, or harmful code. This scan runs in the background without requiring any user intervention. The scan is designed to quickly identify and quarantine malicious files while allowing safe assets to proceed.

Scan Result[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14305753258258-Antivirus-Scan-for-Uploads#h_01J71XQHAZVM685Q2ADRXT2XCT)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Passed Scan**: If the file is clean and passes the antivirus check, it remains in the Waiting Room and can proceed through the regular workflow, including approval and tagging.
*   **Failed Scan:** If the file contains any malicious elements, it will be quarantined and appear next to the asset in the Waiting Room. The file will not be accessible in the system until resolved or deleted.

![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/27567277384466)

Benefits of the Antivirus Scan[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/14305753258258-Antivirus-Scan-for-Uploads#h_01J71XS465TPF6FW5MZ7Z0V1Q2)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Increased Security:** Bynder protects your digital asset library from potential threats, ensuring that uploaded files are safe before they are approved for general use.
*   **Automated Protection: T**he scanning process is automatic, minimizing the risk of human error while maintaining security standards.
*   **Efficient Workflow:** The antivirus scan runs in the background, not disrupting your regular upload and approval workflow.

NOTE: You cannot open the preview of an asset in Quarantine. 

3\. **Infected assets:** Assets in this section have been scanned as malicious.

4\. **Unscanned** **assets**: Assets in this section have not been scanned due to file size (4GB or larger) and will remain in this section until they are either **Allowed** or **Deleted**.

5\. Select the asset(s) and click **Allow** or **Delete**.

*   *   If you click **Allow**, the asset(s) can then be audited in the [**Waiting** **Room**.](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870280)
        
    *   If you click **Delete**, the assets will be deleted from the **Quarantine,** and you’ll be prompted to close the Waiting Room request.

6\. Once an asset is removed from the Quarantine, it must still be reviewed in the Waiting Room before being added to the Asset Bank.
