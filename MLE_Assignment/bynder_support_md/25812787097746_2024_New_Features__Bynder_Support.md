### DAM

### Filter by Date

Introducing a new Smartfilter - [Filter by Date](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/6190351879186-Create-Metaproperties#:~:text=Date,of%20date%20formatting.)
! Easily filter assets by date ranges for date-type metaproperties like “rights expiry,” “event,” or “photo shoot dates.” This tool enhances search precision and is perfect for managing asset rights or tracking seasonal content.
