# View Assets in the Asset Bank – Bynder Support

**Source URL:** https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016051239-View-Assets-in-the-Asset-Bank

The [Asset Bank](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931619)
 provides access to your organization’s most recent and valuable assets. You can [search](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870360)
 for assets and then choose to [share](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013870240)
, [download](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013931219)
, or add them to [a collection](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013932639)
.

By clicking on an asset's thumbnail, you can view the [Asset Detail or Info tab](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/21745299220882)
. This tab displays metaproperties, version history, and usage rights, giving users all the necessary information to manage and use assets efficiently.

Each user's view of the Asset Bank and Asset Detail may vary based on their permissions or account settings. To learn more about Asset Bank Permissions, click [here](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433246503442)
. If necessary, [contact your Bynder Admin](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/19683354865682)
 to gain additional permissions. 

### Note

If you would like to see the asset on a larger scale without clicking into the file, you can enable the [pop-over](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360013869880)
 feature in your portal.

How to View Related Assets[](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016051239-View-Assets-in-the-Asset-Bank#h_01J9C91ZMNGZHJJ00EAKVXBBQQ)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If the [related assets feature is enabled](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/17780673599506)
 in your portal, you can easily view assets that are linked or connected to the one you're currently viewing.

1.  Navigate to the Asset Bank.
2.  Select an asset to open its Asset Detail View.
3.  In the bottom-left corner of the Asset Detail View, you'll see a section labeled **Related** **Files**.![](https://support.bynder.com/hc/article_attachments/21787375100434)
4.  Click on any of the related files, and you'll be directed to the Asset Detail View of the selected asset.
    *   To view archived related assets, the permissions for [View Archived Assets](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433246503442-Permissions-in-Asset-Bank#:~:text=View%20archived%20assets%20%2D%20Allows%20users%20to%20view%20archived%20assets.)
         and [Download Archived Assets](https://support.bynder.com/hc/en-us/articles/18433246503442-Permissions-in-Asset-Bank#:~:text=Download%20archived%20assets%20%2D%20Allows%20users%20to%20download%20archived%20assets.)
         must be enabled.
    *   If a recently uploaded asset does not appear immediately simply refresh your asset overview page.
